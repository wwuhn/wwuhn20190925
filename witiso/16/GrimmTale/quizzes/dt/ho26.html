    a. muscle

  14. There should be ___ to life than just work.
    a. more
    b. mower

    a. missed
    b. mist

    a. tern
    b. turn

    b. ment(a suffix)

  19. She got a topaz ___ from her mother for her birthday.
    a. broach
    b. brooch
