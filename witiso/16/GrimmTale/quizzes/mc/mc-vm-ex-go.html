Expressions with Go

Read the sentences and choose the best alternative.
Then click the answer button to see the correct answer.

  1. Why did he go ___ on his word?
    a. after
    b. back
    c. away

  2. I don't think you should go ___ a job in that company.
    a. after
    b. in
    c. to

  3. Time goes ___ quickly, my dear.
    a. by
    b. for
    c. in

  4. The price of gas did not go ___ as we expected.
    a. off
    b. about
    c. down

  5. My complaint goes ___ you, too.
    a. on
    b. for
    c. in

  6. John is not happy because his son went ___ the Army.
    a. for
    b. forward
    c. into

  7. I believe she'll never go ___ for sewing.
    a. in
    b. down
    c. out

  8. What's going ___ here!
    a. round
    b. in
    c. on

  9. I guess John didn't go ___ well with Mary's parents.
    a. by
    b. to
    c. over

  10. Don't you think we should go ___ our plans again?
    a. down
    b. through
    c. on

  11. Love and hate normally go ___ .
    a. together
    b. about
    c. forth

  12. What he said goes ___ his principles.
    a. against
    b. off
    c. ahead
