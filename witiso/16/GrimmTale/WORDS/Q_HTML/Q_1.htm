quality [(1) that which something is known to have or be; (2) amount of value or excellence] n.(1)性质；（2）质量

An important quality of steel is its strength. (1)
钢铁的一个最重要的性质是它的硬度。

Their goods are of the highest quality. (2)
他们的商品是质量最好的。

question [(1) to ask; (2) to express wonder or disbelief; (3) a sentence or word used in asking for information; (4) a problem; (5) an issue to be discussed] v.（1）询问；（2）怀疑；n.（3）题目；（4）问题；（5）讨论的议题

他父亲向他询问关于那辆车的事。

民权问题在这次运动中是一个重要的议题。
