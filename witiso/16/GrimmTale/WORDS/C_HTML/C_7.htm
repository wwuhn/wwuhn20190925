During a middle age crisis, people often make changes in their life. (1)
在中年这个关键时刻，人们通常做出人生中的转变。

The Cuban missile crisis in the 1960s almost caused a nuclear war. (2)
1960年代的古巴导弹危机几乎引起一场核战争。
