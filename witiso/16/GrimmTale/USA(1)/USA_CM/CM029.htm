English USA Lesson 29：表达愿望。

Conversation Model

Martin:

I want to buy a shirt.

Eileen:

Do you want a shirt for work?

Martin:

No, I want a shirt for tennis.

Eileen:

Do you want a white shirt?

Martin:

I don't know. No, I don't like white.

Eileen:

What color do you want?

Martin:

I don't know. What don't you like?

Martin:

I want some coffee.

Eileen:

I want some coffee too.

Martin:

I want to find a coffee shop.
