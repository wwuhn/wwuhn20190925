English USA Lesson 39：描述人的容貌。

Conversation Model

Waleed:

Martin, does your son play football?

Martin:

He's not on the team. I don't want him to play
football. He's too small.

Waleed:

He isn't very small. He's tall.

Martin:

But he's too thin.

Martin:

What does he look like?

Waleed:

He's not very tall.

Martin:

What color is his hair?

Waleed:

I can't see his hair. He has a cap.
