English USA Lesson 26, Part 2

1.
　

复习Part 1 所学的表达。在上交了驾驶证和汽车申请表后，马丁位新车买了保险。听听马丁如何回答职员的提问。

  L26P2_1.MP3[651KB]

This is English USA, on the Voice of America. Now, Lesson 26, Part 2.

MARTIN:

Excuse me.

CLERK:

May I help you?

MARTIN:

I need insurance on my new car.

CLERK:

Yes, I can help you. Just a minute, please. Now. Tell me your name please.

MARTIN:

My name is Martin Learner.

CLERK:

Do you have insurance with us?

MARTIN:

No, I don't.

CLERK:

Please give me your address.

MARTIN:

Fourteen-twenty-seven, Grant Street, Baltimore, Maryland.

CLERK:

Do you have a telephone?

MARTIN:

Yes, I do. The number is four-one-oh, five-five-five, six-seven-two-nine.

CLERK:

Are you married?

MARTIN:

Yes.

CLERK:

What is your wife's name?

MARTIN:

Eileen Learner.

CLERK:

Do you have children?

MARTIN:

I have two children.

CLERK:

Do they drive?

MARTIN:

My son drives.

CLERK:

How old is your son?

MARTIN:

He's seventeen years old.

CLERK:

What do you do?

MARTIN:

I'm a reporter.

CLERK:

Where do you work?

MARTIN:

I work at the Voice of America.

CLERK:

Where is that?

MARTIN:

It's in Washington, D.C.

CLERK:

Does your wife work?

MARTIN:

Yes, she does.

CLERK:

Does she drive?

MARTIN:

Yes, she does.

CLERK:

How many cars do you have?

MARTIN:

I have only one car.

CLERK:

Is this a new car?

MARTIN:

Yes, it is.

CLERK:

What color is it?

MARTIN:

It's blue.

CLERK:

How many doors does it have? 

MARTIN:

It has four doors.

CLERK:

Do you have a license for the car?

MARTIN:

Yes, I do.

CLERK:

What is the number?

MARTIN:

A-B-C, fifteen-forty.

CLERK:

Did you have an old car?

MARTIN:

Yes, I did.

CLERK:

Did you sell it?

MARTIN:

Yes, I did.

CLERK:

You have a driver's license. 

MARTIN:

Of course.

CLERK:

Have you had any accidents?

MARTIN:

No, I haven't.

 

2.

用“Yes”和“No”回答马丁就你的好恶所作的提问。

  L26P2_2.MP3[209KB]

MARTIN:

Am I Martin Learner?

YOU:

[Yes, you are.]

MARTIN:

Am I a reporter?

YOU:

[Yes, you are.]

MARTIN:

Are you married?

YOU:

[Yes, I am married.] or [No, I am not married.]

MARTIN:

Are you listening to the radio?

YOU:

[Yes, I am listening to the radio.] or [No, I am not listening to the radio.]

MARTIN:

Is your radio new?

YOU:

[Yes, my radio is new.] or [No, my radio is not new.]

MARTIN:

Is today Tuesday?

YOU:

[Yes, it is Tuesday today.] or [No, it is ------ today.]

MARTIN:

Do you have children?

YOU:

[Yes, I have children.] or [No, I don't have children.]

MARTIN:

Do they live with you?

YOU:

[Yes, they live with me.] or [No, they don't live with me.]

MARTIN:

Does your father work?

YOU:

[Yes, he works.] or [No, he doesn't work.]

MARTIN:

Does he have a car?

YOU:

[Yes, he has a car.] or [No, he doesn't have a car.]

3.

用“Yes”和“No”回答马丁就你的好恶所作的提问。

MARTIN:

Were you listening to the radio yesterday?

YOU:

[Yes, I was listening to the radio yesterday.] or [No, I was not listening to the radio yesterday.]

MARTIN:

Were you working last year?

YOU:

[Yes, I was working last year.] or [No, I was not working last year.]

MARTIN:

Was your school in town?

YOU:

[Yes, my school was in town.] or [No, my school was not in town.]

MARTIN:

Was your family with you last month?

YOU:

[Yes, my family was with me last month.] or [No, my family was not with me last month.]

MARTIN:

Did they travel last year?

YOU:

[Yes, they traveled last year.] or [No, they didn't travel last year.]

MARTIN:

Did you see your friends last week?

YOU:

[Yes, I saw my friends last week.] or [No, I didn't see my friends last week.]
