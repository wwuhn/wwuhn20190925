Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 17. First, this noun（名词）:

　

husband

丈夫

　

Here are some adverbs used in giving directions（方位副词）:

　

east / I live east of here.

东方/我住在东边。

west / I live west of here.

西方/我住在西边。

north / I live north of here.

北方/我住在北边。

south / I live south of here.

南方/我住在南边。

　

Next are these adjectives（形容词）:

　

all

所有的
Are all these men farmers?

some

一些
May I ask some questions?

　

Finally, some "Yes" or "No" questions（"Yes" 或 "No" 问与答）:

　

Have we met?

Yes, we have. / No, we haven't.

　

我们见过吗？

对，我们见过。/不，我们没见过。

　

Can you ride?

Yes, I can. / No, I can't.

　

你会骑马吗？

我会。/不，我不会。

　

Are you Lisa's husband?

Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.

　

你是丽莎的丈夫吗？

对，我是。/不，我不是。

　

May I visit your farm?

[Yes, you may. / No, you may not.]

　

我能参观你的农场吗？

可以，你可以参观。/不，你不能参观。
