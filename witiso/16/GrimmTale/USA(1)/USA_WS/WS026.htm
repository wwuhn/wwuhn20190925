Hi. This is Ray McDonald and Nancy Beardsley with words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 26. We begin with some "Yes/No" questions and answers（"Yes/No" 问答 ）:

<Question>

<Answer>

　

Am I a teacher?

Yes, you are./ No, you aren't.

我是一名教师吗？

是的，你是。/不，你不是。

　

Am I a reporter?

Yes, you are./ No, you aren't.

　

你是一名记者吗？

是的，你是。/不，你不是。

　

Are you married?

Yes, I am./ No, I'm not.

　

你结婚了吗？

是的，我结婚了。/不，我还没结婚。

　

Are you listening?

Yes, I am./ No, I'm not.

　

你在听吗？

是的，我在听。/不，我没在听。

　

Is your radio new?

Yes, it is./ No, it isn't.

　

你的收音机是新的吗？

是的，那是新的。/不，它不是新的。

　

Is today Tuesday?

Yes, it is./ No, it isn't.

　

今天是星期二吗？

是的。/不是。

　

Do you have children?

Yes, I do./ No, I don't.

　

你有孩子吗？

是的，我有。/不，我没有。

　

Do they live with you?

Yes, they do./ No, they don't.

　

他们跟你一块住吗？

是的。/不是。

　

Does you father work?

Yes, he does./ No, he doesn't.

　

你父亲工作吗？

是的，他工作。/不，他没有工作。

　

Does she have a car?

Yes, she does./ No, she doesn't.

　

她有车吗？

是的，她有。/不，她没有。

　

Was your school in town?

Yes, it was./ No, it wasn't.

　

你的学校在市里吗？

是的。/不是。

　

Was your family with you?

Yes, they were./ No, they weren't.

　

你的家人跟你在一块吗？

是的。/不是。

　

Were you listening to the radio?

Yes, I was./ No, I wasn't.

　

你在听收音机吗？

是的。/不是。

　

Were you working last year?

Yes, I was./ No, I wasn't.

　

去年你在工作吗？

是的。/不是。

　

Finally, some question words that begin the following sentences（特殊疑问句，用疑问词开头的句子）:

　

What is your name?

你叫什么名字？

　

What do you do?

你是干什么的？

　

Where is he going?

他去哪里？

　

Where does he live?

他住哪？

　

Who is that?

那是谁？

　

Who do you see?

你看见谁？

　

When was he going?

他什么时候去？

　

When did he work?

他什么时候工作的？

　

How was she traveling?

她是怎样旅游的？

　

How did they come here?

他们怎样来这里的？
