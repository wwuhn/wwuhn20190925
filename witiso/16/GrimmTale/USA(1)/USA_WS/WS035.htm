Finally, some notes about time:

　

The easiest way to state exact time according to a clock or watch is to use numbers only. For example:

表达准确时间最简单的方法就是使用数字。例如：

　

9:15 (= nine-fifteen)

9点15分

　

12:30 (= twelve-thirty)

12点半

　

2:24 (= two twenty-four)

2点24分

　

7:51 (= seven fifty-one)

7点51分

　

8:05 (= eight oh-five)

8点5分

　

10:10 (= ten-ten)

10点10分
　

In other words, give the number before the colon and then give the number after the colon. There are other ways to give the time, but this is the easiest and all English speakers will understand it.

换言之，用冒号与数字来表达时间是最简单的方法而且所有使用英语者都能明白。
