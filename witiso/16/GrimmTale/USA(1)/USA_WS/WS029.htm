And finally, some grammar notes（语法说明）:

　

Sentences using "like" and "want" have the same structure but different meanings. Compare the meanings of the following sentences.

使用like与want的句子结构相同但意思不同。比较下列句子的意思。

　

<Want>

<Like>

　

I want a new shirt.

I like the new shirt.

　

我想要一件新衬衣。

我喜欢那件新衬衣。

　

I want a black dress.

I like the black dress.

　

我想要一件黑裙子。

我喜欢那件黑裙子。

　

I want to buy a new shirt.

I like to buy clothes.

　

我想买一件新衬衣。

我喜欢买衣服。

　

I want to play tennis.

I like to play tennis.

　

我想打网球。

我喜欢打网球。
