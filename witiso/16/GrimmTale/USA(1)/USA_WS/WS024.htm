Hi. This is Ray McDonald and Nancy Beardsley with words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 24. We begin with a list of nouns（名词）:

airplane

飞机

　

camp

阵营，露营地

　

coach

教练

　

football

足球

　

mountains

山 [mountain]

　

plane

飞机

　

runner

奔跑者

　

seat belt

（汽车、收音机等的）座椅安全带

　

track

径类运动

　

train

火车

　

Here are some verbs from our lesson（动词）:

　

coach

教导、训练

　

drink

喝

　

fasten

拴紧

　

fly

飞行

　

have

有

　

Next are some adjectives（形容词）:

　

bad

坏的

　

nervous

紧张的

　

slow

慢的

　

Finally, some sentences that show the difference between the uses of "going" （显示"going"区别的一些例句）:

　

I am going to Dallas. [noun]

我将要去达拉斯。（going + 名词）

　

I am going to write. [verb]

我要去写字。（going + 动词）

　

I am going to school. [noun]

我要去上学。（going + 名词）

　

I am going to play. [verb]

我要去玩。（going + 动词）
