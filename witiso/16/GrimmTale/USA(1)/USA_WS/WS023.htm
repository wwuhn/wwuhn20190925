Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 23. First, these nouns（名词）:

problem

问题

　

swim meet

游泳比赛

　

Here is an adverb（副词）:

　

later

后来，之后

See you later.

　

A verb is next（动词):

　

finish

完成、结束

　

Next are some phrases at the end of a conversation:

结束对话时使用的短语：

　

See you later.

待会见。

　

　

　

　

See you on Saturday.

星期六见。

　

Sure. (= yes)

好的。

　

And finally, making statements about the future:

对未来进行描述：

　

Are you going to play?

你要去玩吗？

　

Yes, I am going to play.

对，我要去玩。

　

　

　

　

Is she going to play?

她要去玩吗？

　

Yes, she is going to play.

对，她要去玩。

　

　

　

　

Are we going to play?

我们要去玩吗？

　

Yes, we are going to play.

对，我们要去玩。

　

　

　

　

Are they going to play?

他们要去玩吗？

　

Yes, they are going to play.

对，他们要去玩。
