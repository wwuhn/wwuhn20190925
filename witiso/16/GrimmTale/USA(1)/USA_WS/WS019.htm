Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 19. First, these nouns（名词）:

　

aunt

阿姨

　

business

商业

　

family

家族

　

grandparent

祖父母

　

housewife

主妇

　

parent

父母

　

family reunion

家族集会

　

salesman

售货员

　

seed

种子

　

Next are two adjectives（形容词）:

　

more

更多的
May I ask some more questions?

any

任何的
Do you have any brothers?

　

Here is an adverb and an example of its use（副词）:

　

only / I have only one sister.

只有，仅有/我只有一个姐妹。

　

Infinitives usually are formed by adding the present tense of a verb to the preposition "to". For example:

不定式（infinitive）就是在介词to后加动词原形。

　

I want to meet Mr. Kessler.

我想见一下克斯勒先生。

　

I need to see her.

我要见她。

　

I like to sing.

我喜欢唱歌。
