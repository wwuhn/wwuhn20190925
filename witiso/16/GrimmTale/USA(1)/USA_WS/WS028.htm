Hi. This is Ray McDonald and Nancy Beardsley with words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 28. We begin with some nouns（名词）:

　

air

空气、大气

guide

指导

　

bug

昆虫

　

ocean

海，海洋

　

forest

森林、山林

　

Here are some adjectives（形容词):

　

clean

清洁的
I like clean air.

　

hot

热的
I don't like hot places.

　

fast

迅速的
We are going to have a fast walk.

　

This is an adverb and an example of its use（副词用法）:

　

too / We won't walk too fast.

太/我们不会走得太快的。

　

Here is a preposition（介词）:

　

near

・・・附近的
I live near the park.

　

Finally, some grammar notes（语法说明）:

　

English sometimes uses an impersonal sentence if the subject is not very important. Compare the following sentences.

在英语里当主语不是很重要时会使用无人称句。比较下列句子。

　

There is one person here. = One person is here.

　

这里有一个人。

　

There are three rivers in the park. = Three rivers are in the park.

　

公园时里有三条河。
