Hi. This is Ray McDonald and Nancy Beardsley with words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 10. We begin with a list of nouns（名词）:

　

airport

 机场

　

map

 地图

　

roads

 道路

　

school

 学校

　

sign

 标志

　

These two adverbs tell where（地点副词）:

　

here

这里
We are here.

　

there

那里
Turn and go there.

　

Next, some sentences in present tense and past tense（现在时与过去时）:

　

<Present: 现在>

<Past: 过去>

　

I live in Baltimore now.

I lived in Washington last year.

　

我现在住在巴的摩亚。

去年我住在华盛顿。

　

I work in Baltimore now.

I worked in Cracow in 1985.

　

我现在在巴的摩亚工作。

`1985年我在克华考工作。

　

I study in Seattle.

I studied in Chicago before.

　

我在西雅图学习。

我以前在芝加哥学习过。

　

Finally, some other sentences in present and future tense（现在时与将来时）:

　

<Present: 现在>

<Future: 将来>

　

Martin is writing a story.

Martin is going to write a story.

　

马丁在写一篇故事。

马丁打算写一篇故事。

　

Martin has a car.

Martin is going to have a car.

　

马丁有一辆车。

马丁将会有一辆车。

　

Martin is in Baltimore.

Martin is going to Seattle.

　

马丁在巴的摩亚。

马丁将要去西雅图。
