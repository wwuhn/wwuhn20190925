MARTIN:

Hello! Hello! Is this the police? Hello. What? Is this the police? Good. I'm Martin Learner. Martin, M-A-R-T-I-N. Learner, L-E-A-R-N-E-R. Yes. Martin Learner! What? My car. . . My car is missing! Missing! It is not here! At the hotel. The Denver Hotel. My car is not here! Come to the police station? O.K.

　

　

喂，喂，是警察局吗？喂，什么？这里是警察局吗？好，我叫马丁・勒纳。马丁，M-A-R-T-I-N。勒纳，L-E-A-R-N-E-R。是的。马丁・勒纳！什么？我的汽车。我的车丢了，丢了！不在这里！在宾馆！丹佛宾馆！我的车不在这里！到警察局来？好吧。

　

　

　

　

DESK CLERK:

Good morning, Mr. Learner. May I help you?

　

　

早上好，勒纳先生。我能为你做点什么？

　

MARTIN:

I'm going to the police.

　

　

我要去警察局。

　

CLERK:

About your car?

　

　

关于你的车吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes. Where is the police station?

　

　

是的。去警察局怎么走？

　

CLERK:

The police station!? I don't know. Wait a moment.

　

　

警察局？我不知道。你等一下。

　

CLERK:

Mr. Hudson. Where is the police station? It's Mr. Learner. His car is missing. Thank you, Mr. Hudson.

　

　

哈德逊先生，去警察局怎么走？勒纳先生的车丢了。谢谢你，哈德逊先生。

　

CLERK:

The Police Station is on Johnson Street.

　

　

警察局在约翰逊街。

　

MARTIN:

Where is Johnson Street?

　

　

约翰逊街怎么走？

　

CLERK:

Go straight ahead for three streets. That's Johnson Street. Turn left.

　

　

一直往前走，过三个街口。 那就是约翰逊街，然后往左转。

　

　

　

　

POLICEWOMAN:

Tell me where you live, please.

　

　

请告诉我你住在哪里。

　

MARTIN:

I live at fourteen-twenty-seven Grant Street. Baltimore, Maryland.

　

　

我住马里兰州，巴尔的摩市，格兰特街，1427号。

　

WOMAN:

Fourteen-twenty-seven Grant Street, Baltimore, Maryland. Give me your phone number.

　

　

马里兰州，巴尔的摩市，格兰特街，1427号。告诉我你的电话号码。

　

MARTIN:

Four-one-oh, five-five-five, six-seven-two-nine.

　

　

410-555-6729。

　

WOMAN:

Please show me your driver's license.

　

　

请出示你的驾驶执照。

　

MARTIN:

Here.

　

　

这里。

　

WOMAN:

O.K.

　

　

好的。

　

WOMAN:

Tell me what happened.

　

　

告诉我事情的经过。

　

MARTIN:

I went to the garage to get my car. I'm going home today. I'm a reporter--

　

　

我去车库开车，今天我要回家。我是一名记者。

　

WOMAN:

Stop! Slow down. Now, begin again.

　

　

等一下！慢下来。现在，重新开始。

　

　

　

　

DETECTIVE:

Good morning, Nikki.

　

　

早上好，尼金。

　

WOMAN:

Good morning, Sam. Come here. Sam, this is Mr. Learner. This is Detective Sam Brook.

　

　

早上好，山姆。过来，山姆，这是勒纳先生。这位是山姆・布鲁克侦探。

　

DETECTIVE:

I'm looking into your missing car.

　

　

我正调查你丢车这个案子。

　

WOMAN:

Sit down, Sam.

　

　

请坐，山姆。
