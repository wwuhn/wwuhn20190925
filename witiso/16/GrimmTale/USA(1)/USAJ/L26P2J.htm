MARTIN:

Excuse me.

　

　

打扰一下。

　

CLERK:

May I help you?

　

　

有什么可以帮忙的吗？

　

MARTIN:

I need insurance on my new car.

　

　

我需要给我的新车投保。

　

CLERK:

Yes, I can help you. Just a minute, please. Now. Tell me your name please.

　

　

好的，我可以帮你。请稍等。请告诉我你的名字。

　

MARTIN:

My name is Martin Learner.

　

　

我叫马丁・勒纳。

　

CLERK:

Do you have insurance with us?

　

　

你以前在我们这里投过保吗？

　

MARTIN:

No, I don't.

　

　

没有。

　

CLERK:

Please give me your address.

　

　

请说一下你的住址。

　

MARTIN:

Fourteen-twenty-seven, Grant Street, Baltimore, Maryland.

　

　

马里兰州，巴尔的摩市，格兰特街，1427号。

　

CLERK:

Do you have a telephone?

　

　

你有电话吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I do. The number is four-one-oh, five-five-five, six-seven-two-nine.

　

　

有。号码是410-555-6729。

　

CLERK:

Are you married?

　

　

你结婚了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes.

　

　

是的。

　

CLERK:

What is your wife's name?

　

　

你太太的名字？

　

MARTIN:

Eileen Learner.

　

　

艾琳・勒纳。

　

CLERK:

Do you have children?

　

　

你有孩子吗？

　

MARTIN:

I have two children.

　

　

我有两个。

　

CLERK:

Do they drive?

　

　

他们也开车吗？

　

MARTIN:

My son drives.

　

　

我儿子会开车。

　

CLERK:

How old is your son?

　

　

你儿子多大了？

　

MARTIN:

He's seventeen years old.

　

　

17岁。

　

CLERK:

What do you do?

　

　

你是做什么的？

　

MARTIN:

I'm a reporter.

　

　

我是记者。

　

CLERK:

Where do you work?

　

　

你的工作单位？

　

MARTIN:

I work at the Voice of America.

　

　

美国之音。

　

CLERK:

Where is that?

　

　

在哪里？

　

MARTIN:

It's in Washington, D.C.

　

　

华盛顿特区。

　

CLERK:

Does your wife work?

　

　

你太太也工作吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, she does.

　

　

是的。

　

CLERK:

Does she drive?

　

　

她会开车吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, she does.

　

　

会。

　

CLERK:

How many cars do you have?

　

　

你有几辆车？

　

MARTIN:

I have only one car.

　

　

只有一辆。

　

CLERK:

Is this a new car?

　

　

这是一辆新车吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, it is.

　

　

是的。

　

CLERK:

What color is it?

　

　

什么颜色？

　

MARTIN:

It's blue.

　

　

蓝色。

　

CLERK:

How many doors does it have?

　

　

有几个车门？

　

MARTIN:

It has four doors.

　

　

四个。

　

CLERK:

Do you have a license for the car?

　

　

你有驾照吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I do.

　

　

有。

　

CLERK:

What is the number?

　

　

编号？

　

MARTIN:

A-B-C, fifteen-forty.

　

　

ABC1540。

　

CLERK:

Did you have an old car?

　

　

你有旧车吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I did.

　

　

有。

　

CLERK:

Did you sell it?

　

　

你把它卖掉了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I did.

　

　

是的。

　

CLERK:

You have a driver's license.

　

　

你有驾照。

　

MARTIN:

Of course.

　

　

当然。

　

CLERK:

Have you had any accidents?

　

　

你出过交通事故吗？

　

MARTIN:

No, I haven't.

　

　

没有。
