MARTIN:

Hello, Lee. How are you?

　

　

嗨，李。你还好吗？

　

LEE:

Fine thanks. How are you?

　

　

很好，谢谢。你好吗？

　

MARTIN:

Fine thanks. You're early. Who was that young man with you?

　

　

很好，谢谢。你来得很早。和你一起的哪个年轻人是谁？

　

LEE:

He's my son.

　

　

是我儿子。

　

MARTIN:

How old is he? He's very big.

　

　

他多大了？长得很高。

　

LEE:

He's fifteen. He wants to play football.

　

　

十五岁。他想踢足球。

　

MARTIN:

Does he play now?

　

　

他现在踢足球吗？

　

LEE:

Yes, he does. He plays in school. He's sitting with my brother.

　

　

是的。他在学校踢。他和我兄弟坐在一起。

　

MARTIN:

Which man is your brother?

　

　

哪一个是你兄弟。

　

LEE:

He has curly brown hair.

　

　

头发是褐色卷曲的那个。

　

MARTIN:

Is he on the left?

　

　

他坐左边吗？

　

LEE:

Yes, he's the one.

　

　

对。

　

MARTIN:

His hair is black.

　

　

他的头发是黑色的。

　

LEE:

No, it's dark brown. Here's Waleed.

　

　

不，是深棕色。瓦立德来了。

　

WALEED:

Hi.

　

　

嗨。

　

MARTIN:

Good afternoon, Waleed.

　

　

下午好，瓦立德。

　

LEE:

How are you?

　

　

你好吗？

　

WALEED:

Fine thanks. How are you, Lee?

　

　

很好，谢谢。你呢，瓦立德？

　

LEE:

Fine.

　

　

很好。

　

WALEED:

How are you, Martin?

　

　

你还好吗，马丁？

　

MARTIN:

I'm OK. Sit here, Waleed.

　

　

我还可以。坐这里，瓦立德。

　

WALEED:

Thanks. Who is that?

　

　

谢谢。那是谁？

　

LEE:

Which one?

　

　

哪一个？

　

WALEED:

Do you see that very tall man?

　

　

看到那个高个男人了吗？

　

LEE:

I see two tall men. Which one?

　

　

我看到两个高个男人，你说哪一个？

　

WALEED:

He has light brown hair.

　

　

头发是浅棕色的那个。

　

LEE:

Does he have very big feet?

　

　

眼睛很大的那个吗？

　

WALEED:

Yes, he does! He has very big feet.

　

　

是的，确实是，他的眼睛很大。

　

LEE:

Is that the coach?

　

　

那个是教练吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, it is.

　

　

对。

　

WALEED:

Where is the other coach?

　

　

另外一个教练在哪里？

　

LEE:

He's over there. Do you see that man with blond hair?

　

　

在那边。看见金色头发的那个人了吗？

　

WALEED:

Is he short?

　

　

个字很矮吗？

　

LEE:

Yes. He has a black book.

　

　

对。他拿了一本黑色的书。

　

WALEED:

Yes, I see. Is your son here?

　

　

对，我看见了。你儿子在这里吗？

　

LEE:

Yes, he is. He's sitting with my brother.

　

　

对，在这里。他和我兄弟坐在一起。

　

WALEED:

Where?

　

　

在哪？

　

LEE:

Over there.

　

　

那边。

　

WALEED:

What does he look like?

　

　

他长什么样子？

　

MARTIN:

He's big.

　

　

很壮实。

　

WALEED:

How old is he?

　

　

他多大了？

　

LEE:

He's fifteen years old.

　

　

十五岁。

　

MARTIN:

He has dark brown hair.

　

　

头发是深棕色的。

　

LEE:

No, he doesn't. He has black hair. My brother has dark brown hair.

　

　

不，不是深棕色，是黑色。我兄弟的头发是深棕色。

　

MARTIN:

He has straight black hair.

　

　

他的头发是黑色的直发。

　

WALEED:

Is it long?

　

　

长的吗？

　

LEE:

It's not very long.

　

　

不是很长。

　

WALEED:

What does your brother look like?

　

　

你兄弟长什么样？

　

MARTIN:

He has curly brown hair.

　

　

棕色卷曲的头发。

　

LEE:

That's right.

　

　

没错。

　

WALEED:

I see them. You son is very handsome.

　

　

我看到他了。你儿子很英俊。

　

LEE:

Thanks. He wants to play football.

　

　

谢谢。他想踢足球。

　

WALEED:

Lee, does you son play football now?

　

　

李，你的儿子现在踢足球吗？

　

LEE:

Yes, he does. He plays in high school.

　

　

是的，他在中学踢足球。

　

MARTIN:

Does he want to go to college?

　

　

他想上大学吗？

　

LEE:

Yes, he does. He wants to play college football.

　

　

是的。他想在大学踢足球。

　

WALEED:

Lee, who is that woman with your brother?

　

　

李，和你兄弟在一起的那个女人是谁？

　

LEE:

I can't see. What does she look like?

　

　

我没看见。她长什么样？

　

WALEED:

She has long blonde hair.

　

　

她的头发是金色的，长长的。

　

MARTIN:

She's very tall and thin.

　

　

她很高，很苗条。

　

LEE:

Is she pretty?

　

　

她长得漂亮吗？

　

WALEED:

She's very pretty.

　

　

非常漂亮。

　

MARTIN:

She's sitting behind your brother.

　

　

她坐在你兄弟后面。

　

LEE:

Oh, I see. That's my sister.

　

　

 哦，我看到了。那是我妹妹。
