English USA Translation
　

L37P2J.MP3[506KB]

　

ALAN:

Hi, dad. Where is Sue?

　

　

嗨，爸爸。秀在哪里？

　

MARTIN:

She's with your mother. They went swimming.

　

　

她和你妈妈在一起。她们去游泳啦。

　

ALAN:

I'm too late?

　

　

我迟到了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, you are.

　

　

是的。

　

ALAN:

Where are they?

　

　

她们在哪里？

　

MARTIN:

Look out there. Can you see them?

　

　

看那里。能看到她们吗？

　

ALAN:

Where?

　

　

哪儿？

　

MARTIN:

Can you see two people there?

　

　

能看到那边两个人吗？

　

ALAN:

Maybe. Oh, yes. I see Sue's blonde hair.

　

　

也许吧。啊，对。我看到了秀的金色头发。

　

MARTIN:

Sue's a good swimmer.

　

　

她游泳非常棒。

　

ALAN:

And mother is too.

　

　

妈妈也是。

　

MARTIN:

Who were those girls?

　

　

那些女孩是谁？

　

ALAN:

Which girls?

　

　

哪些女孩？

　

MARTIN:

The girls over there. You were talking to them.

　

　

那边的那些。你刚才同她们说过话的。

　

ALAN:

They're friends.

　

　

她们是朋友。

　

MARTIN:

Are they from your school?

　

　

她们也是你们学校的吗？

　

ALAN:

Some are from school.

　

　

有一些是。

　

MARTIN:

Which ones?

　

　

哪些？

　

ALAN:

Do you see the tall girl? She has black hair. She has a small head and big brown eyes.

　

　

你看到那个高个女孩了吗？她的头发是黑色的。她的头小，眼睛很大，是棕色的。

　

MARTIN:

Were you talking to her?

　

　

你和她说过话吗？

　

ALAN:

Yes, I was.

　

　

是的。

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I see her.

　

　

对，我看见她了。

　

ALAN:

She's in my class at school. Do you see that woman behind the girls?

　

　

她在我们班上。你看到哪些女孩后面的那位女士了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Is she sitting?

　

　

是坐着的吗？

　

ALAN:

Yes, she is. She's sitting with a little boy. She's that girl's mother. And the boy is her brother.

　

　

对。她和一个小男孩坐在一起。她是那女孩的妈妈。那男孩是她的弟弟。

　

MARTIN:

Where is the father?

　

　

她爸爸在哪里？

　

ALAN:

He's bringing food. Do you see that short girl?

　

　

他买吃的去了。你看见那个矮个的女孩了吗？

　

MARTIN:

The one with brown hair?

　

　

头发是棕色的那个吗？

　

ALAN:

No, the one with blonde hair.

　

　

不是，是金色的。

　

MARTIN:

Yes. She's standing beside the tall girl.

　

　

是的。她站在高个女孩旁边。

　

ALAN:

She's in my school too. She's not in my class. Her mother and father are here too. Do you know them?

　

　

她也是我们学校的，但不是我们班的。她爸爸和妈妈也在这里。你认识他们吗？

　

MARTIN:

I don't know. Who are they?

　

　

不认识。她们是谁？

　

ALAN:

Mr. and Mrs. Kovisar.

　

　

科维萨先生和太太。

　

MARTIN:

I know the name. Where are they?

　

　

我知道他们的名字。他们在哪里？

　

ALAN:

Mr. Kovisar is that fat man.

　

　

科维萨先生是那个胖男人。

　

MARTIN:

He's not very fat! He's only big.

　

　

他没有那么胖!只是有点胖。

　

ALAN:

He's fat. He's short and fat.

　

　

他胖。他又矮又胖。

　

MARTIN:

Which woman is Mrs. Kovisar?

　

　

科维萨太太是哪一个？

　

ALAN:

I don't see her.

　

　

我没看到她。

　

MARTIN:

Who are the other girls?

　

　

另外哪些女孩是谁？

　

ALAN:

They're not from my school. I only met them today. Do you want to swim?

　

　

她们不是我们学校的。我今天才碰到她们。你想游泳吗？

　

MARTIN:

Maybe later. You go. Sue likes swimming with you.

　

　

也许以后吧。秀喜欢和你一起游泳。

　

ALAN:

OK. See you later.

　

　

好吧。呆会儿见。
