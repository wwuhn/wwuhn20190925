English USA Translation

CARA:

That was beautiful. Did you like it, Martin?

　

　

很美，你喜欢吗，马丁？

　

MARTIN:

It was wonderful.

　

　

的确非常好。

　

CARA:

Did you like it, Howard?

　

　

你喜欢吗，霍华德？

　

HOWARD:

Of course.

　

　

当然喜欢。

　

CARA:

Now, Howard, which one is your sister?

　

　

霍华德，哪一个是你妹妹？

　

HOWARD:

That woman playing the violin.

　

　

正在拉小提琴的那个。

　

CARA:

Which one?

　

　

哪一个？

　

HOWARD:

She has a black dress.

　

　

她穿着黑衣服。

　

MARTIN:

They all have black dresses.

　

　

他们都穿黑衣服。

　

HOWARD:

She has red hair.

　

　

她的头发是红色的。

　

CARA:

Oh, I see. She has beautiful hands. She has long fingers.

　

　

哦，我看到了。她的手很美，手指很修长。

　

MARTIN:

Yes, she does. I like her hair. Your hair isn't red.

　

　

没错。我喜欢她的头发。你的头发不是红色的。

　

HOWARD:

Our father's hair is red. He doesn't like his red hair.

　

　

我父亲的头发是红色的，但他却不喜欢。

　

CARA:

Does your sister like her hair?

　

　

你妹妹喜欢红头发吗？

　

HOWARD:

Yes, she does.

　

　

是的，她喜欢。

　

CARA:

It's very nice.

　

　

非常漂亮。

　

MARTIN:

Is her violin old?

　

　

她的小提琴很旧了吗？

　

HOWARD:

Yes, it is. It's very old.

　

　

是的，已经很旧了。

　

CARA:

Martin, who is that man over there?

　

　

马丁，那边那个男人是谁？

　

MARTIN:

Which man?

　

　

哪一个？

　

CARA:

He's sitting behind that tall man.

　

　

坐在那个高个男人后面的那个。

　

MARTIN:

The man with white hair?

　

　

白头发的那个吗？

　

CARA:

No, no. Look to the right. Do you see that tall man?

　

　

不是。往右看。你看到那个高个男人了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Maybe.

　

　

也许吧。

　

CARA:

Who is the man sitting behind the tall man?

　

　

坐在那个高个男人后面的人是谁？

　

MARTIN:

I can't see his face.

　

　

我看不到他的脸。

　

CARA:

Now look.

　

　

现在看看。

　

HOWARD:

Is he the man with the large head?

　

　

是那个大头的人吗？

　

CARA:

Yes. He has a round face and brown hair.

　

　

是的。他长着圆脸和棕色的头发。

　

MARTIN:

He's a Senator.

　

　

他是一个议员。

　

HOWARD:

What's his name?

　

　

他叫什么名字？

　

MARTIN:

I don't know.

　

　

不知道。

　

CARA:

Where is he from?

　

　

他从哪里来？

　

MARTIN:

I don't know.

　

　

不知道。

　

　

　

　

MARTIN:

Your sister is wonderful, Howard.

　

　

你妹妹真棒，霍华德。

　

CARA:

Yes, she is. She's very pretty too.

　

　

对。她也很漂亮。

　

HOWARD:

Thank you.

　

　

谢谢。

　

CARA:

Is she very tall?

　

　

她很高吗？

　

HOWARD:

Yes, she is.

　

　

是的。

　

CARA:

She has long arms and long fingers.

　

　

她的胳膊和手指都很长。

　

MARTIN:

You're not tall. Is your father tall?

　

　

你不高。你父亲高吗？

　

HOWARD:

No, he's short. My mother is short. I'm short too. But my sister and brother are tall.

　

　

不，他不高。我母亲也矮。我也很矮。但我兄弟和妹妹都高。

　

CARA:

What color are her eyes?

　

　

她的眼睛是什么颜色的？

　

HOWARD:

They are blue.

　

　

蓝色。

　

CARA:

I like red hair and blue eyes.

　

　

我喜欢红头发蓝眼睛。

　

HOWARD:

Don't you like my hair?

　

　

你不喜欢我的头发？

　

MARTIN:

You don't have any hair.

　

　

你没有头发。

　

HOWARD:

I have some hair.

　

　

我还是有一些头发的。

　

CARA:

You have very nice blue eyes too.

　

　

你的眼睛也是漂亮的蓝色。

　

　

　

　

MARTIN:

Howard, who is that woman?

　

　

霍华德，那位女士是谁？

　

HOWARD:

Which one?

　

　

哪一个？

　

CARA:

Shhhh. Be quiet.

　

　

嘘。安静。
