MARTIN:

Thank you for talking with me, Daoud.

　

　

谢谢你和我谈话，达欧德。

　

DAOUD:

You're welcome. These are my friends. This is Juma. And this is Mona.

　

　

不客气。这些事我的朋友。这是朱莫。这是莫娜。

　

MARTIN:

Hello. I'm Martin Learner.

　

　

嗨。我是马丁・勒纳。

　

DAOUD:

Martin is writing a story about me. He's a reporter.

　

　

马丁正在写一篇关于我的报道。他是一名记者。

　

MONA:

Hi.

　

　

嗨。

　

JUMA:

Hello. What newspaper do you write for?

　

　

嗨。你是哪家报社的？

　

MARTIN:

I'm not a newspaper reporter. I'm a radio reporter.

　

　

我不是报社记者。我是电台记者。

　

JUMA:

Oh, I see. Do you like tennis?

　

　

哦，我知道了。你喜欢网球吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I do. I play tennis. But I don't play very well. Do you play tennis?

　

　

是的。我打网球。但我打得不太好。你喜欢打网球吗？｡

　

JUMA:

No, I don't.

　

　

我不喜欢。

　

MARTIN:

Mona, do you like tennis?

　

　

莫娜，你喜欢网球吗？

　

MONA:

I like to watch. I don't play. Daoud is the only tennis player.

　

　

我喜欢看，但我不打。只有达欧德打网球。

　

MARTIN:

Daoud, do you like other sports?

　

　

达欧德，你还喜欢其他运动吗？

　

DAOUD:

I like some sports.

　

　

我喜欢一些运动。

　

MARTIN:

What sports do you like?

　

　

那你喜欢什么运动？

　

DAOUD:

I like to watch track. I like the running and jumping.

　

　

我喜欢看田径赛。我喜欢跑步和跳高。

　

MARTIN:

Do you like track, Juma?

　

　

你喜欢田径吗，朱娜？

　

JUMA:

Yes, I do. I was a runner in school.

　

　

喜欢。我在学校历史赛跑运动员。

　

MARTIN:

Do you run now?

　

　

你现在还跑吗？

　

JUMA:

No, I don't. I like to watch running.

　

　

不跑了。我喜欢看别人跑。

　

MARTIN:

Do you like other sports, Mona?

　

　

你还喜欢其他运动吗，莫娜？

　

MONA:

I like to watch. I don't do anything.

　

　

我喜欢看。我什么都不做。

　

MARTIN:

You don't swim, or run, or play tennis?

　

　

你不游泳，或跑步，或者打网球吗？

　

MONA:

No, I don't do anything.

　

　

不，我什么都不做。

　

MARTIN:

What do you like to watch?

　

　

你喜欢看什么？

　

MONA:

I like to watch tennis. I like basketball.

　

　

我喜欢看网球赛。还喜欢篮球。

　

MARTIN:

What other sports do you play, Daoud?

　

　

达欧德，你还做什么运动？

　

DAOUD:

I run.

　

　

我跑步。

　

MARTIN:

Do you like running?

　

　

你喜欢跑步吗？

　

DAOUD:

Sometimes. I run every day. It's good for my tennis.

　

　

有时候喜欢。我每天都跑步。这对打网球很好。

　

JUMA:

I run with Daoud sometimes.

　

　

我有时和达欧德去跑步。

　

MARTIN:

Do you run, Mona?

　

　

莫娜，你跑步吗？

　

MONA:

No, I don't run.

　

　

不，我不跑步。

　

MARTIN:

What do you like to do?

　

　

你喜欢做什么？

　

MONA:

I like to read. I like to cook. I like to travel. I like to shop.

　

　

我喜欢看书，做饭，旅游，逛街。

　

JUMA:

Yes, she likes to shop.

　

　

对，她喜欢逛街。

　

MARTIN:

What do you like to shop for?

　

　

你喜欢买什么东西？

　

MONA:

I like to shop for clothes. Daoud likes to shop too.

　

　

你喜欢买衣服。达欧德也喜欢逛街。

　

MARTIN:

What do you like to shop for, Daoud?

　

　

达欧德，你喜欢买什么？

　

DAOUD:

I like to shop for clothes too.

　

　

我也喜欢买衣服。

　

MARTIN:

Daoud, you're a very good tennis player. When did you begin?

　

　

达欧德，你的网球打得很好。你什么时候开始打的？

　

DAOUD:

I began when I was 10 years old. I lived in Virginia. I began in school.

　

　

从十岁那年开始打。那是我住在弗吉尼亚。我在学校里开始打的。

　

MARTIN:

Did you like to play tennis?

　

　

那时你喜欢打网球吗？

　

DAOUD:

Sometimes.

　

　

有时。

　

MARTIN:

You liked to play sometimes?

　

　

那时你有时候喜欢？　

　

DAOUD:

I liked other sports too. I liked swimming in the summer. And I liked running. And other things.

　

　

我还喜欢其他运动。我喜欢夏天游泳，还喜欢跑步，还有其他运动。

　

MARTIN:

What tennis players do you like, Daoud?

　

　

达欧德，你喜欢那些网球运动员？　

　

DAOUD:

I like many tennis players. I like Jimmy Connors. I like Andre Agassi.

　

　

我喜欢许多网球运动员。我喜欢吉米・卡诺斯，还有安德鲁・阿嘎西。

　

MARTIN:

What tennis players do you like, Mona?

　

　

莫娜，你喜欢那些运动员？

　

MONA:

I like Daoud.

　

　

我喜欢达欧德。

　

JUMA:

We all like Daoud!

　

　

我们都喜欢达欧德！
