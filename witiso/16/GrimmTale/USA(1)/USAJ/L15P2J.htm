MARTIN:

Mayor Walker, how many small businesses do you have?

　

　

沃克市长，你们有多少家商号？

　

MAYOR:

About two hundred twenty. People work in stores and shops. They work in banks. They work in restaurants and bars. They work in the Post Office. They work in bakeries.

　

　

大约220家。人们在商店、店铺银行、饭店、酒吧、邮局和面包店上班。

　

MARTIN:

What do they make in the bakeries?

　

　

面包店里做什么？

　

MAYOR:

They bake bread and cakes. Do you see that man there?

　

　

他们烤面包和蛋糕。你看到那边那个人了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes.

　

　

看到了。

　

MAYOR:

He's a writer.

　

　

他是一个作家。

　

MARTIN:

Where does he write?

　

　

他写什么？

　

MAYOR:

He writes for the newspaper.

　

　

他为报纸写稿。

　

MARTIN:

I see a photographer.

　

　

我看到了一个摄影师。

　

MAYOR:

Yes, she works for the newspaper.

　

　

对，她为报社工作。

　

MARTIN:

Who are those women?

　

　

那些女人是谁？

　

MAYOR:

They're workers.

　

　

她们是工人。

　

MARTIN:

Where do they work?

　

　

她们在哪里上班？

　

MAYOR:

They work at the plastic factory.

　

　

在塑料厂。

　

MARTIN:

What do they make?

　

　

她们造什么？

　

MAYOR:

They make plastic bags.

　

　

造塑料袋。

　

MARTIN:

Who are those men?

　

　

那些男人是谁？

　

MAYOR:

They're workers too.

　

　

他们也是工人。

　

MARTIN:

Where do they work?

　

　

他们在哪里上班？

　

MAYOR:

They work at the tire factory.

　

　

他们在轮胎厂。

　

MARTIN:

Who are they?

　

　

他们是谁？

　

MAYOR:

They're students.

　

　

是学生。

　

MARTIN:

Where do they study?

　

　

他们在哪里上学？

　

MAYOR:

They study at the high school.

　

　

在中学。

　

MARTIN:

They're good musicians too! Who are those children?

　

　

他们也是优秀的音乐人！那些孩子们是谁？

　

MAYOR:

They're students too. They're going to sing.

　

　

他们也是学生。他们要唱歌。

　

MAYOR:

Do you see those men?

　

　

你看到那些人了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes. Who are they?

　

　

是的。他们是谁？

　

MAYOR:

They're carpenters.

　

　

他们是木匠。

　

MARTIN:

Oh, yes. I see.

　

　

啊，是的，我看到了。

　

MAYOR:

We have many carpenters.

　

　

我们有很多木匠。

　

MARTIN:

Those men work on the roads and streets.

　

　

那些人在道路和街道上工作。

　

MAYOR:

Yes.

　

　

对。

　

MARTIN:

Who are those women?

　

　

那些妇女是谁？

　

MAYOR:

They're workers.

　

　

她们是工人。

　

MARTIN:

Where do they work?

　

　

她们在哪里上班？

　

MAYOR:

They work in the glass factory.

　

　

在玻璃厂。

　

MARTIN:

What do they make?

　

　

她们制造什么？

　

MAYOR:

They make windows and windshields.

　

　

她们做窗户和挡风玻璃。

　

MARTIN:

Where do they work?

　

　

他们在哪里上班？

　

MAYOR:

They work in the bottle factory.

　

　

在瓶子厂。

　

MARTIN:

What do they make?

　

　

他们做什么？

　

MAYOR:

They make bottles.

　

　

造瓶子。

　

MARTIN:

Where do they work?

　

　

他们在哪里上班？

　

MAYOR:

They work in the tire factory.

　

　

在轮胎厂。

　

MARTIN:

What do they make?

　

　

他们造什么？

　

MAYOR:

They make tires.　Where do you work?

　

　

他们造轮胎？你在哪里上班？

　

MARTIN:

I work at the Voice of America. Thank you for your time, Mayor Walker.

　

　

我在美国之音电台工作。谢谢你的时间，沃克市长。
