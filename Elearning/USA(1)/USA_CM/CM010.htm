English USA Lesson 10：看地图问路。

Conversation Model

  Conversation Model

Martin:

Excuse me, where is the airport?

Woman:

It's on Nixon Road.

Martin:

Where is Nixon Road? I have a map.

Woman:

Good. Look here. We are here.

Martin:

I see.

Woman:

Now look there. That's the airport.

Man:

Turn right. Then go two roads.

Martin:

I have a map. Show me the school.

Woman:

Go to Nixon Road.

Martin:

I have a map. Show me the airport.
