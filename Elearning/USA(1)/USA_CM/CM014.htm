English USA Lesson 14：询问工作时间、地点。

Conversation Model

Martin:

When do you work?

Ralph:

I work on Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday.

Martin:

Celia, when do you work?

Celia:

I work on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.

Martin:

Ralph, where do you work?

Ralph:

I work at the Frontier Restaurant.
