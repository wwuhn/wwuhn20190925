Lesson 1, Part 1

CHARLES:

Mary! Mary Scott!

玛丽！玛丽・斯科特。

MARY:

Charles? Hello, Charles.

查尔斯？嗨，查尔斯。

CHARLES:

Good morning. How are you?

早上好。你还好吗？

MARY:

Fine thanks. How are you?

很好，谢谢。你好吗？

CHARLES:

Fine thanks.

很好，谢谢。

MARY:

What are you doing here?

你在这里做什么？

CHARLES:

I'm meeting Martin Learner.

我来接马丁・勒纳。

MARY:

Who is Martin Learner?

马丁・勒纳是谁？

CHARLES:

He's a reporter with the Voice of America.

他是美国之音电台的记者。

MARY:

A reporter?

记者？

CHARLES:

Yes. He's visiting our new airport.

是的。他要来参观我们的新机场。

PA VOICE:

Mr. Martin Learner. Martin Learner. Please come to the information desk.

马丁・勒纳先生。马丁・勒纳。请到咨询台。

PA VOICE:

Mr. Martin Learner. Please come to the information desk.

马丁・勒纳先生，请到咨询台。

MARTIN:

Good morning. I'm Martin Learner.

早上好，我是马丁・勒纳

INFO CLERK:

Good morning, Mr. Learner. That man is waiting for you.

早上好，勒纳先生。那位先生正在等您。

MARTIN:

Thank you.

谢谢。

INFO CLERK:

You're welcome.

不客气。

MARTIN:

Hello. I'm Martin Learner.

你好，我是马丁・勒纳。

CHARLES:

Good morning, Mr. Learner. I'm Charles Bishop.

早上好，马丁・勒纳先生。我是查尔斯・贝舍普。

Hello. How are you?

嗨，你好吗？

CHARLES:

Fine thanks.

很好，谢谢。

MARY:

I'm Mary Scott. How are you?

我叫玛丽・斯科特。你好吗？

MARTIN:

Fine thanks.

很好，谢谢。
