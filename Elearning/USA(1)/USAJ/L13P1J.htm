KATHY:

Good morning, Jeff.

　

　

早上好，杰夫。

　

JEFF:

Good morning, Kathy. How are you this morning?

　

　

早上好，凯西。你今天上午好吗？

　

KATHY:

Fine thanks. How are you?

　

　

很好，谢谢。你好吗？

　

JEFF:

Fine thanks.

　

　

很好，谢谢。

　

KATHY:

What's that?

　

　

那是什么？

　

JEFF:

It's the plan.

　

　

是设计图。

　

KATHY:

What plan?

　

　

什么设计图？

　

JEFF:

For the new building. Look.

　

　

新楼的设计图。看。

　

KATHY:

It's interesting. What's that?

　

　

有意思。那是什么？

　

JEFF:

Shops. There are ten shops.

　

　

店铺。有十家店铺。

　

KATHY:

What's that?

　

　

那是什么？

　

JEFF:

It's the cinema.

　

　

是电影院。

　

KATHY:

Show me the restaurants.

　

　

请把饭店指给我看。

　

JEFF:

Look here. There are three restaurants.

　

　

看这儿。有三家饭店。

　

KATHY:

Show me the sports club.

　

　

把运动俱乐部指给我看。

　

JEFF:

Look here.

　

　

看这里。

　

　

　

　

KATHY:

Martin Learner is coming this morning.

　

　

马丁・勒纳今天上午要来。

　

JEFF:

Martin Learner?

　

　

马丁・勒纳

　

KATHY:

He's a reporter.

　

　

他是一位记者。

　

JEFF:

Oh, yes. He's a reporter for the Voice of America.

　

　

啊，对。他是美国之音的记者。

　

KATHY:

Yes.

　

　

是的。

　

　

　

　

JEFF:

Please come in, Mr. Learner.

　

　

勒纳先生，请进。

　

MARTIN:

Thank you.

　

　

谢谢。

　

JEFF:

I'm Jeff O'Neil. I'm the director of the shopping mall. How are you?

　

　

我叫杰夫・欧尼尔，是购物中心的主管。你还好吗？

　

MARTIN:

Fine thanks.

　

　

很好，谢谢。

　

JEFF:

Please sit down. Coffee?

　

　

请坐。喝咖啡吗？

　

MARTIN:

No, thanks.

　

　

不用，谢谢。

　

JEFF:

Where are you from?

　

　

你从哪里来？

　

MARTIN:

I'm from Baltimore, Maryland.

　

　

我来自马里兰州的巴尔的摩市。

　

JEFF:

I lived in Maryland. How can I help you?

　

　

我以前住在马里兰州。要我怎样帮你呢？

　

MARTIN:

Tell me about the shopping mall. May I record?

　

　

跟我说说购物中心。我可以录音吗？

　

JEFF:

Sure. That's O.K. Look here.

　

　

当然可以。好的。看这里。

　

MARTIN:

What's that?

　

　

那是什么？

　

JEFF:

It's the plan for the new building.

　

　

那是新楼的计划图。

　

MARTIN:

Show me the old building.

　

　

给我看看旧楼。

　

JEFF:

Here is the old building. Here is the new building.

　

　

这是旧楼。这里是新楼。

　

MARTIN:

What's that?

　

　

那是什么？

　

JEFF:

It's the cinema.

　

　

那是电影院。

　

MARTIN:

Show me the shops.

　

　

给我看看店铺。

　

JEFF:

Here is a shoe store. Here is a bicycle shop. Here is a gift shop. Here is a clothing store. There are ten shops.

　

　

这里是一个鞋店，这里是一个自行车店，这里是一个礼品店，这里是一个服装店。一共有十个。

　

MARTIN:

What's that?

　

　

那是什么？

　

JEFF:

It's the sports club.

　

　

那是运动俱乐部。

　

MARTIN:

What's that?

　

　

那是什么？

　

JEFF:

It's a restaurant. There are three restaurants.

　

　

那是一个饭店。有三家饭店。

　

MARTIN:

Show me.

　

　

指给我看看。

　

JEFF:

Here is a restaurant. Here is a restaurant. And here is a restaurant.

　

　

这里是一个饭店，这里是一个，这里还是一个。

　

MARTIN:

Please show me the shopping mall.

　

　

请给我看看购物中心。

　

JEFF:

Come with me. First, we're going to look at the new building.

　

　

跟我来。我们先去看看新楼。
