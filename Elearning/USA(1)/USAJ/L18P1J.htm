English USA Translation

LISA:

Let's sit here, Mr. Learner.

　

　

勒纳先生，我们坐在这里吧。

　

MARTIN:

Please, call me Martin. May I ask some questions?

　

　

请叫我马丁好了。我能问你几个问题吗？

　

LISA:

Of course.

　

　

当然。

　

MARTIN:

May I record your answers?

　

　

我可以把你的回答录下来吗？

　

LISA:

Yes.

　

　

可以。

　

MARTIN:

Do you have a family?

　

　

你结婚了吗？

　

LISA:

Yes, I have a husband.

　

　

是的。我有丈夫。

　

MARTIN:

What is your husband's name?

　

　

你丈夫叫什么名字？

　

LISA:

Eric.

　

　

埃里克。

　

MARTIN:

Eric. That's right. We've met.

　

　

埃里克。是的，我们见过。

　

MARTIN:

Do you have children?

　

　

你有孩子吗？

　

LISA:

Yes. We have two children.

　

　

是的。有两个。

　

MARTIN:

Are they boys or girls?

　

　

男孩还是女孩？

　

LISA:

We have one boy and one girl.

　

　

一个男孩，一个女孩。

　

MARTIN:

Wonderful. You have a son and a daughter. Are your mother and father living?

　

　

真好。你有一个儿子，一个女儿。你的父母还健在吗？

　

LISA:

Yes, they are.

　

　

是的。

　

MARTIN:

Do they live here?

　

　

他们住在这里吗？

　

LISA:

No, they don't. They live in town.

　

　

不，他们住在城里。

　

MARTIN:

Do you have a large farm?

　

　

你的农场大吗？

　

LISA:

Oh, yes. It's a very large farm. The farm was my father's farm.

　

　

大，很大。那个农场是我父亲的。

　

MARTIN:

And you have a very large house.

　

　

你的房子很大。

　

LISA:

It's a wonderful house. The house is old. Here's Eric.

　

　

那是一所很漂亮的房子，也很老。埃里克来了。

　

ERIC:

Hello. Martin?

　

　

嗨，你是马丁？

　

MARTIN:

That's right.

　

　

对。

　

ERIC:

How are you?

　

　

你好吗？

　

MARTIN:

Fine thanks. How are you?

　

　

很好，谢谢。你呢？

　

ERIC:

Fine thanks.

　

　

很好，谢谢。

　

LISA:

We're talking about families.

　

　

我们在谈论家。

　

ERIC:

I have a large family.

　

　

我家人很多。

　

MARTIN:

Are your parents living?

　

　

你父母还在吗？

　

ERIC:

My mother is living. My father isn't living.

　

　

我母亲还在，父亲已经过世了。

　

MARTIN:

Where does your mother live?

　

　

你母亲住在哪里？

　

ERIC:

She lives in California.

　

　

他住在加利福尼亚。

　

MARTIN:

Do you have brothers and sisters?

　

　

你有兄弟姐妹吗？

　

ERIC:

I have three brothers and two sisters.

　

　

我有三个兄弟，两个姐妹。

　

MARTIN:

Where do they live?

　

　

他们都住哪里？

　

ERIC:

Two brothers live in town. One lives on a farm.

　

　

两个兄弟住城里。另一个住农场里。

　

MARTIN:

What do they do? One is a farmer?

　

　

他们都是做什么的？有一个是农场主吗？

　

ERIC:

That's right. Two brothers have businesses in town.

　

　

对。两个兄弟在城里有自己的生意。

　

MARTIN:

Lisa, do you have brothers and sisters?

　

　

利萨，你有兄弟姐妹吗？

　

LISA:

I have a sister.

　

　

我有一个姐妹。

　

MARTIN:

Where does she live?

　

　

他住哪里？

　

LISA:

She lives in Texas.

　

　

住在德克萨斯。

　

MARTIN:

Is she married?

　

　

她结婚了吗？

　

LISA:

No, she isn't.

　

　

没有。

　

MARTIN:

What does she do?

　

　

她是做什么的？

　

LISA:

She's in the army.

　

　

她在部队。

　

MARTIN:

Are your sisters married, Eric?

　

　

埃里克，你的姐妹都结婚了吗？

　

ERIC:

One sister lives in Montana. She's married to a rancher. One lives in California. My mother lives with this sister.

　

　

有一个姐妹住在蒙大纳，她嫁给了一个牧场主。一个住在加利福尼亚。我母亲和她住一起。

　

MARTIN:

What does this sister do?

　

　

你的这个姐妹是做什么的？

　

ERIC:

She has a business.

　

　

她有自己的生意。

　

LISA:

She's a photographer.

　

　

她是摄影师。

　

MARTIN:

That's interesting.

　

　

真不错。

　

　

　

　

LISA:

Eric, where are our children?

　

　

埃里克，孩子们在哪里？
