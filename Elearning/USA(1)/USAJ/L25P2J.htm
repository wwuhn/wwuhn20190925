English USA Translation
　

L25P2J.MP3[657KB]

　

MARTIN:

When did you come to the United States?

　

　

你什么时候来的美国？

　

FEMALE:

I came last month.

　

　

上个月。

　

MARTIN:

What are you going to do in New York?

　

　

你在纽约打算干什么？

　

FEMALE:

I'm going to visit friends. And I'm going to visit hospitals.

　

　

我打算看朋友，还要去参观医院。

　

MARTIN:

When do you visit family?

　

　

你什么时候去看你的家人？

　

FEMALE:

I don't have any family in America.

　

　

我的家人都不在美国。

　

MARTIN:

When do you visit family in Georgia?

　

　

你什么时候去乔治亚看你家人？

　

FEMALE:

I visit family during my vacation.

　

　

我在假期去看家人。

　

MARTIN:

When is your vacation?

　

　

你的假期在什么时候？

　

FEMALE:

Sometimes my vacation is in the summer.

　

　

有时我在夏天休假。

　

MARTIN:

When did you visit your family?

　

　

你上次是什么时候看望你的家人？

　

FEMALE:

I visited my family last summer. I worked in Kiev last year. I visited my family during my vacation.

　

　

是去年夏天。去年我在基辅工作。我在休假中去看了我的家人。

　

MARTIN:

What do you like to do during your vacation?

　

　

你休假时喜欢做什么？

　

FEMALE:

I like to swim. I like to walk in the mountains.

　

　

我喜欢游泳。我喜欢到山上散步。

　

MARTIN:

Do you have mountains in Georgia?

　

　

乔治亚有山吗？

　

FEMALE:

Of course. We have many mountains and rivers. When do you go on vacation?

　

　

当然有。我们那里有很多山川河流。你什么时候休假？

　

MARTIN:

I go on vacation during the summer. Sometimes I go during the winter.

　

　

我在夏季休假。有时我也在冬季休假。

　

FEMALE:

What do you like to do?

　

　

你喜欢做什么？

　

MARTIN:

I like to walk in the mountains too. My wife likes to hear music and see plays.

　

　

我也喜欢到山上走走。我太太喜欢听音乐，看戏剧。

　

FEMALE:

When does she see plays?

　

　

她什么时候看戏剧？

　

MARTIN:

She went to see a play last week.

　

　

她上周还去看了一场。

　

FEMALE:

Where did she see the play?

　

　

她在哪里看的？

　

MARTIN:

She saw the play in Baltimore. We live in Baltimore. She saw a play in New York too.

　

　

在巴尔的摩。我们住在巴尔的摩。她也在纽约看过一次。

　

FEMALE:

When?

　

　

什么时候？

　

MARTIN:

Last month.

　

　

上个月。

　

FEMALE:

Do you like music?

　

　

你喜欢音乐吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I do.

　

　

喜欢。

　

FEMALE:

When do you listen to music?

　

　

你一般什么时候听音乐？

　

MARTIN:

I listen on the radio. I go to concerts.

　

　

我听收音机，还去听音乐会。

　

FEMALE:

When do you go to concerts?

　

　

你什么时候去音乐会？

　

MARTIN:

I went to a concert last month.

　

　

我上个月去的。

　

FEMALE:

Do you like sports?

　

　

你喜欢运动吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I do. I like baseball. Do you like baseball?

　

　

喜欢。我喜欢棒球。 你喜欢棒球吗？

　

FEMALE:

No, I don't. When do you go to baseball?

　

　

我不喜欢。你什么时候去看棒球？

　

MARTIN:

We say baseball games.

　

　

我们把它叫做“棒球赛”。

　

FEMALE:

You go to baseball games.

　

　

你去看棒球赛。

　

MARTIN:

That's right.

　

　

没错。

　

FEMALE:

When do you go to baseball games?

　

　

你什么时候去看棒球赛？

　

MARTIN:

In the summer.

　

　

夏天。

　

FEMALE:

Did you go last summer?

　

　

去年夏天你去了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Of course. I went with my family. I went with my friends too.

　

　

当然。我和我家人去的。我还和我的朋友去过。

　

FEMALE:

Do you like reading?

　

　

你喜欢看书吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I do.

　

　

喜欢。

　

FEMALE:

I like reading very much.

　

　

我非常喜欢看书。

　

MARTIN:

When do you like to read?

　

　

你喜欢什么时候看书？

　

FEMALE:

I like to read in the winter. I read Tolstoy in Kiev.

　

　

我喜欢冬天读。我在基辅看托尔斯泰的书。

　

MARTIN:

When did you read Tolstoy?

　

　

你什么时候看过托尔斯泰的书？

　

FEMALE:

Last winter.

　

　

去年冬天。

　

MARTIN:

Do you read English books?

　

　

你看英文书吗？

　

FEMALE:

Sometimes. I like American books. I read Mark Twain.

　

　

有时。我喜欢美国的书。我看马克・吐温的书。

　

MARTIN:

When did you read Mark Twain?

　

　

你什么时候看过他的书？

　

FEMALE:

Oh-- Fifteen years ago. I was in school.

　

　

哦，15年前。那时我还在上学。

　

FEMALE:

When did you play baseball?

　

　

你什么时候打过棒球？

　

MARTIN:

Last summer. I played with my son and his friends. Do you like to travel?

　

　

去年夏天。我和我儿子还有他的朋友打棒球。你喜欢旅游吗？

　

FEMALE:

Yes, I do. I meet new friends. I study. I work with new children. I like to travel.

　

　

喜欢。我可以看望朋友，学习，和新认识的孩子们工作。我喜欢旅游。

　

MARTIN:

Do you like sports?

　

　

 你喜欢运动吗？

　

FEMALE:

Yes, I do. I like to play tennis.

　

　

我喜欢。我喜欢打网球。

　

MARTIN:

I like to play tennis too. When do you play?

　

　

我也喜欢打网球。你什么时候打网球？

　

FEMALE:

I play when I travel. I played in Atlanta. I'm going to play in New York.

　

　

我旅游时打。我在亚特兰大打过。我要去纽约打。

　

MARTIN:

Would you like some coffee?

　

　

你想喝点咖啡吗？

　

FEMALE:

No, thank you. I don't like coffee.

　

　

不用，谢谢。我不喜欢咖啡。
