MARTIN:

Manny, where do you live in Bloomington?

　

　

曼尼，你住布龙明堂哪里？

　

MANNY:

I live at five twenty-six Hamilton Street.

　

　

我住汉密尔顿街526号。

　

MARTIN:

Do you live in a house?

　

　

是单独一家吗？

　

MANNY:

No, I don't.

　

　

没有。

　

MARTIN:

Do you live in a dormitory?

　

　

你住宿舍吗？

　

MANNY:

No, I don't.

　

　

不是。

　

MARTIN:

Do you live in an apartment?

　

　

你住公寓吗？

　

MANNY:

Yes, I do. It's very small.

　

　

是的，但很小。

　

MARTIN:

How many rooms does it have?

　

　

有几个房间？

　

MANNY:

It has one large room and one very small room.

　

　

一个大房和一个小房间。

　

MARTIN:

That was beautiful.

　

　

不错。

　

MANNY:

He's very good.

　

　

他很棒。

　

MUSICIAN:

Thanks.

　

　

谢谢。

　

MARTIN:

Manny, what do you do?

　

　

曼尼，你是做什么的？

　

MANNY:

I'm a musician too.

　

　

我也是音乐人。

　

MARTIN:

Are you a student?

　

　

你是学生吗？

　

MANNY:

Yes, I am. I study at the university.

　

　

是的。我读大学。

　

MARTIN:

Do you play the violin?

　

　

你拉小提琴吗？

　

MANNY:

No, I don't.

　

　

不是。

　

MUSICIAN:

Yes, he does.

　

　

他是。

　

MANNY:

No, I don't.

　

　

不是。

　

MUSICIAN:

Yes, he does. But he's very bad.

　

　

他是，但他的水平不高。

　

MANNY:

Yes, I'm very bad.

　

　

是的，我水平很低。

　

MARTIN:

Where are you from?

　

　

你从哪里来？

　

MANNY:

I'm from Brooklyn. My family is from Russia. It was their home.

　

　

我从布鲁克林来。我父母是俄罗斯人，那里是他们的家。

　

MARTIN:

Did you live in Russia?

　

　

你在俄罗斯住过吗？

　

MANNY:

No, I didn't. My home was in Brooklyn.

　

　

没有。我家在布鲁克林。

　

MANNY:

They lived in Russia. It was their home.

　

　

他们住过俄罗斯，那是他们的家。

　

MARTIN:

Where are you from?

　

　

你从哪里来？

　

MANNY:

I'm from Brooklyn. It was my home. I live in Bloomington now.

　

　

我从布鲁克林来，那里是我的家。我现在住在那里。

　

MUSICIAN:

I'm from Finland. It was my home. I live in Bloomington too.

　

　

我从芬兰来。那里是我的家。我现在也住在布龙明堂。

　

MANNY:

Where are you from?

　

　

你从哪里来？

　

MARTIN:

I'm from Chicago. It was my home. I live in Baltimore now.

　

　

芝加哥。那里是我的家现在我住在巴尔的摩。

　

MARTIN:

Did you play the violin in Brooklyn?

　

　

你在布鲁克林演奏过小提琴吗？

　

MANNY:

Yes. I was very bad.

　

　

是的，但很糟糕。

　

MARTIN:

What do you play now?

　

　

你现在演奏什么？

　

MANNY:

I play the clarinet.

　

　

单簧管。

　

MARTIN:

Did your father play the clarinet?

　

　

你父亲是吹单簧管的吗？

　

MANNY:

No, he didn't. He was a chemist.

　

　

不是。他以前是一名药剂师。

　

MARTIN:

Did your mother play the clarinet?

　

　

你母亲是吹单簧管的吗？

　

MANNY:

No, she didn't. But she was a musician. She was a singer.

　

　

不是，但她以前是搞音乐的。她是唱歌的。

　

MARTIN:

Do you play another musical instrument?

　

　

你还演奏什么乐器吗？

　

MANNY:

Yes, I do. I play the guitar.

　

　

是的，我还弹吉他。

　

MUSICIAN:

He plays the mandolin too.

　

　

他还弹曼陀林。

　

MUSICIAN:

Excuse me. Teresa! Hi.

　

　

不好意思。特里萨！嗨。

　

TERESA:

Hi.

　

　

嗨。

　

MUSICIAN:

This is Teresa.

　

　

这是特里萨。

　

MANNY:

Hi, Teresa.

　

　

嗨，特里萨。

　

MARTIN:

Hello. I'm Martin. Are you a musician?

　

　

你好，我是马丁。你是搞音乐的吗？

　

TERESA:

No. I'm a teacher.

　

　

不是，我是教师。

　

MUSICIAN:

She teaches at the high school.

　

　

她教中学。

　

MARTIN:

Do you live in Bloomington?

　

　

你住在布龙明堂吗？

　

TERESA:

Yes, I do.

　

　

是的。

　

MUSICIAN:

She lives in the apartment downstairs.

　

　

她住在市区的公寓。

　

TERESA:

Manny, do you have your guitar?

　

　

曼尼，你带你的吉他了吗？

　

MANNY:

Yes, I do.

　

　

带了。

　

TERESA:

Play for us.

　

　

给我们弹弹吧。

　

MANNY:

OK.

　

　

好吧。
