English USA Translation
　

L25P1J.MP3[449KB]

　

FEMALE:

Good afternoon. May I sit here?

　

　

早上好。我可以坐在这里吗？

　

MARTIN:

Of course. Please.

　

　

当然。请坐。

　

FEMALE:

I am Dr. Darejan Mamoulishvili.

　

　

我是达勒詹・玛牟立史威利医生。

　

MARTIN:

I'm Martin Learner.

　

　

我是马丁・勒纳。

　

FEMALE:

Are you going to New York, Mr. Learner?

　

　

勒纳先生，你要去纽约吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I am. I have some work there.

　

　

是的。我在那里有些工作要做。

　

FEMALE:

What do you do?

　

　

你是做什么的？

　

MARTIN:

I'm a reporter.

　

　

我是记者。

　

FEMALE:

Very nice.

　

　

非常好。

　

MARTIN:

What do you do?

　

　

你是做什么的？

　

FEMALE:

I'm a doctor.

　

　

我是医生。

　

MARTIN:

Are you a medical doctor?

　

　

你是医生吗？

　

FEMALE:

Yes. I'm a children's doctor.

　

　

是的。我是儿科医生。

　

MARTIN:

Wonderful! Are you going to New York?

　

　

真棒！你要去纽约吗？

　

FEMALE:

Yes, I am.

　

　

是的。

　

MARTIN:

Do you have work there?

　

　

你到那里是去工作吗？

　

FEMALE:

No, I don't. I am on vacation.

　

　

不是。我去度假。

　

MARTIN:

Where are you from?

　

　

你是哪里人？

　

FEMALE:

I am from Georgia.

　

　

我从乔治亚来。

　

MARTIN:

Are you from Atlanta? You don't sound Georgian.

　

　

你是亚特兰大人吗？你听上去不向乔治亚人。

　

FEMALE:

I am not from Georgia in America. I am from Georgia in Europe.

　

　

我不是从美国的乔治亚来。我从欧洲的乔治亚来。

　

MARTIN:

Where are you from in Georgia?

　

　

你从乔治亚的什么地方来？

　

FEMALE:

I'm from Tbilisi.

　

　

第比利斯。

　

MARTIN:

Do you work in Tbilisi?

　

　

你在那里工作吗？

　

FEMALE:

Sometimes. I worked in Moscow last month. I worked in Kiev last year.

　

　

有时。我上个月在莫斯科工作。去年在基辅工作。

　

MARTIN:

Do you have friends in New York?

　

　

你在纽约有朋友吗？

　

FEMALE:

Yes, I do. I worked in New York.

　

　

有。我在纽约工作过。

　

MARTIN:

How long did you work in New York?

　

　

你在那里工作了多长时间？

　

FEMALE:

Only one year.

　

　

只有一年。

　

MARTIN:

When did you work in New York?

　

　

是什么时候？

　

FEMALE:

Five years ago. But I visited New York too.

　

　

五年前。但除此之外，我还来过纽约。

　

MARTIN:

When did you visit New York?

　

　

你什么时候去过纽约？

　

FEMALE:

Ten years ago. Three years ago. And last year.

　

　

十年前，三年前，还有去年。

　

MARTIN:

Who do you visit?

　

　

你去看谁？

　

FEMALE:

I visit my doctor friends. I visit hospitals.

　

　

我去看我的医生朋友，还有医院。

　

MARTIN:

You have a working vacation.

　

　

你边工作边休假。

　

FEMALE:

That's right. I was working in Atlanta.

　

　

对。我以前在亚特兰大工作。

　

MARTIN:

Atlanta, Georgia? Georgia in the United States?

　

　

乔治亚的亚特兰大吗？是美国的乔治亚吗？

　

FEMALE:

That's right. Georgia in America.

　

　

是的。美国的乔治亚。

　

MARTIN:

When were you in Georgia?

　

　

你什么时候在乔治亚？

　

FEMALE:

I was in Georgia last week. I visited the Centers for Disease Control.

　

　

上个星期。我去参观了那里的疾病控制中心。

　

MARTIN:

Who did you visit?

　

　

你去看谁了？

　

FEMALE:

It was a meeting. I met with children's doctors. We talked about children's diseases.

　

　

那是一个会议。我和儿科医生会面了。我们谈论了儿科疾病。

　

MARTIN:

When did you meet?

　

　

你们什么时间的面？

　

FEMALE:

We met last Thursday and Friday.

　

　

上周四和周五。
