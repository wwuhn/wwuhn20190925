EILEEN:

Did you finish your book, Sue?

　

　

你看完了书吗，秀？

　

SUE:

Yes, I did.

　

　

看完了。

　

ALAN:

Did you like it? I read that book four years ago.

　

　

喜欢吗？我四年前读的那本书。

　

SUE:

Yes, I liked it.

　

　

是的，我喜欢。

　

ALAN:

That book is for boys.

　

　

那本书是给男孩子读的。

　

SUE:

No, it isn't.

　

　

不是。

　

ALAN:

Yes, it is.

　

　

是。

　

EILEEN:

No, it isn't, Alan. I read that book many years ago.

　

　

不是，艾伦。我很多年前读过那本书。

　

ALAN:

Dad?

　

　

爸爸？

　

MARTIN:

Don't ask me. I don't want to say. I read it too. Alan, when are you coming home?

　

　

别问我。我不想说。我也读过。艾伦，你什么时候回家？

　

ALAN:

In the morning. At nine o'clock.

　

　

早上。9点钟。

　

EILEEN:

Are you going to have breakfast?

　

　

你打算吃早饭吗？

　

ALAN:

Yes.

　

　

是的。

　

EILEEN:

When are you going to have breakfast?

　

　

你打算什么时候吃？

　

ALAN:

We're going to have breakfast there, in the morning. When is your meeting, dad?

　

　

我们打算在哪里吃早饭，早上。你什么时候开会，爸爸？

　

MARTIN:

The meeting is at eleven o'clock.

　

　

11点。

　

ALAN:

May I come home at eleven o'clock? You can meet me at the Itoh's house.

　

　

我11点来可以吗？你可以到依钭的房里找我。

　

MARTIN:

No. You come home at nine o'clock. I want you to come before my meeting.

　

　

不，你9点钟回家。我想让你在我开会之前回来。

　

ALAN:

OK.

　

　

好的。

　

SUE:

Alan, can you come to the movies with us?

　

　

艾伦，你能和我一起看电影吗？

　

ALAN:

When are you going to the movies?

　

　

你打算什么时候去？

　

SUE:

At four o'clock.

　

　

4点。

　

ALAN:

What movie are you going to see?

　

　

你要看什么电影？

　

SUE:

We're going to see the Walt Disney movie.

　

　

我们要看瓦特・迪斯尼电影。

　

ALAN:

That's for children.

　

　

那是小孩子看的。

　

SUE:

Daddy's going with me.

　

　

爸爸和我一起去。

　

ALAN:

I don't want to see a movie for children.

　

　

我不想看小孩子的电影。

　

EILEEN:

Alan, it's not for children.

　

　

艾伦，那不是给小孩子看的。

　

ALAN:

I have to study in the afternoon.

　

　

我下午得学习。

　

SUE:

Can you study in the evening?

　

　

你晚上学行吗？

　

ALAN:

I have to study in the afternoon and in the evening.

　

　

我必须在下午和晚上学习。

　

SUE:

When are you going to study?

　

　

你什么时候学习？。

　

ALAN:

I'm going to study after lunch and after dinner.

　

　

我打算午饭和晚饭后学。

　

EILEEN:

When are you going to work?

　

　

你什么时候工作？

　

ALAN:

I'm not going to work tomorrow. I have to work on Monday.

　

　

明天我不工作。我周一得工作。

　

EILEEN:

When are you going to work on Monday?

　

　

你周一什么时候干活？

　

ALAN:

At seven-thirty.

　

　

7：30。

　

MARTIN:

In the evening?

　

　

晚上吗？

　

ALAN:

No. In the morning.

　

　

不，早上。

　

EILEEN:

Before school?

　

　

去上学前？

　

ALAN:

Of course. I'm going to work at seven-thirty and then I'm going to school at eight-thirty.

　

　

当然。我7：30干活，然后8：30去上学。

　

MARTIN:

I don't like you to work in the morning.

　

　

我不想让你早上干活。

　

ALAN:

I'm going to work only this week.

　

　

我只是这周干活。
