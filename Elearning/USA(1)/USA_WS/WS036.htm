Finally, we offer these grammar notes.

　

Many verbs in English are made of two words, a verb plus a preposition or adverb of place. These are sometimes called idioms. 许多英语动词后加介词或副词。

Some examples:

　

come / come back

来/回来。

　

clean / clean up

清洁/打扫干净

　

Meanings of the verb and the two-word verb are related but slightly different in meaning. Learn the two-word verbs as separate vocabulary and meaning.单个动词与动词短语在意思上会有所不同，所以要分开学习。
