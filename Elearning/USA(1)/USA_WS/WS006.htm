Hi. This is Ray McDonald and Nancy Beardsley with words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 6. We begin with a question word（疑问词）:

　

where   

哪里      Where is the museum?
               博物馆在那里？

　

Next, some verbs（动词）:

　

ask （及物或不及物）

问

　

go

去

　

come

来

　

turn

转弯

　

Here are some adverbs that often show direction or location（ 以下是些表示方向或位置的副词）:

　

right

右边
Turn right. 向右转。

　

upstairs

楼上
Go upstairs. 上楼。

　

left

左边
Turn left.向左转。

　

downstairs

楼下
Go downstairs.下楼。

　

straight ahead

直向前方
Go straight ahead.一直向前走。

　

there

那里
Look there.瞧那儿。

　

Another verb in Lesson 6（本课动词）:
　

know / I don't know.

知道/我不知道。

　

These are some nouns（名词）:

　

building

建筑物

　

museum

博物馆、美术馆

　

director

馆长

　

river

河流

　

Next, a pronoun（代词）:

　

it / It's on the river.

它/它在河上。
Where is the museum? It's on the river.

　

Next, a preposition（介词）:

　

on / on the river

在...之上/在河上

　

Finally, these two phrases（短语）:

　

Excuse me.

劳驾。

　

Sorry. (I'm sorry.)

对不起。
