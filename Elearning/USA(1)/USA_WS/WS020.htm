Finally, some verbs that use different words for past tense（过去时与现在时不同的动词）:

　

<Present: 现在时>

<Past: 过去时>

　

I go to high school.

I went to high school last year.

　

我上中学。

我去年上中学。

　

I sit here.

I sat there last year.

　

我坐在这里。

我去年坐在那里。

　

I have a car.

I had a truck last year.

　

我有一辆车。

我去年有一辆卡车。
