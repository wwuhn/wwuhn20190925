English USA Lesson 38, Part 2

1.
　

学习对人物的描写。马丁与朋友出席音乐会，休息时与观众一起聊天。

L38P2_1.MP3[653KB]

This is English USA, on the Voice of America. Now, Lesson 38, Part 2.

CARA:

Howard, you sister is very beautiful. Is your brother handsome?

HOWARD:

Of course. We're all handsome. But he has hair.

MARTIN:

Would you like a drink?

CARA:

Yes, please. Anything.

HOWARD:

May I help?

MARTIN:

No, thanks. I'm going to get them.

CARA:

Howard, do you see that large woman?

HOWARD:

The one behind Martin?

CARA:

No, no. The one with brown hair.

HOWARD:

The one with the white dress?

CARA:

Yes. Who is she?

HOWARD:

She works in our building?

CARA:

Where does she work?

HOWARD:

Upstairs. She knows Martin. They're talking.

CARA:

Do you know her?

HOWARD:

Yes. She's very interesting.

MARTIN:

How are you, Helen?

HELEN:

Fine thanks. Do you like the concert?

MARTIN:

Yes. Very much. Do you like the music?

HELEN:

Yes, I do. Who is that woman with Howard?

MARTIN:

She's Cara Gutierrez.

HELEN:

She has a very nice face. But she's very thin.

*1

MARTIN:

Come meet her.

MARTIN:

Here you are.

CARA:

Thank you, Martin.

HOWARD:

Thanks.

MARTIN:

Cara, this is Helen Stockdale.

CARA:

Hello. I'm Cara Gutierrez.

HELEN:

Martin told me. I'm happy to meet you. Hello, Howard. How are you?

HOWARD:

Fine thanks. How are you?

HELEN:

Fine thanks. Do you like the concert, Howard?

HOWARD:

I love it.

CARA:

His sister is playing.

HELEN:

That's wonderful. Which one is she?

MARTIN:

She plays the violin.

HOWARD:

She's very tall. She has red hair and blue eyes.

CARA:

She has long arms and long fingers.

HELEN:

Yes, I know.

CARA:

Helen, do you know that man? He has gray hair and gray eyes.

HELEN:

I don't see him.

CARA:

He's behind that fat woman.

HELEN:

Is she short?

CARA:

Yes. She has a very round face.

MARTIN:

What color are her eyes?

CARA:

I can't see her eyes.

HELEN:

Does she have black hair?

CARA:

Yes, she does.

HELEN:

That's Mrs. Van Wolk. She has a cafe on M Street.

CARA:

Oh, yes. Now I see.

HELEN:

I don't know the man.

MARTIN:

Is your friend tall?

YOU:

[Yes, my friend is tall.] or [No, my friend is not tall.]

MARTIN:

Are you tall?

YOU:

[Yes, I am tall.] or [No, I am not tall.]

MARTIN:

What color is your friend's hair?

YOU:

[The color of my friend's hair is --------.]

MARTIN:

What color is your hair?

YOU:

[The color of my hair is -------.]

MARTIN:

Is your friend's face round?

YOU:

[Yes, my friend's face is round.] or [No, my friend's face is not round.]

MARTIN:

Is your face round?

YOU:

[Yes, my face is round.] or [No, my face is not round.]
