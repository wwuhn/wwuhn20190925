under [(1) below; (2) below the surface of; (3) less than; (4) as called for by a law, agreement or system] prep.(1)在...之下；（2)在...之内；（3）低于；（4）假借

The river flows under the bridge. (1)
河水在桥底下流淌。

The submarine went under. (2)
潜艇沉到水下。

The book was under 20 dollars. (3)
这本书的价格在二十美金以下。

Such action is not permitted under the law. (4)
这种行为是法律不允许的。
