  1. A: I heard we would have so much snow in Michigan. Is that right?

    B: Yes, it is. Actually it starts to snow from October, and in January we ___ have so much snow.

    a. will
    b. do
    c. does

  2. A: Didn't you get lost in Detroit?

    B: No, I didn't . But I ___ get lost in Ann Arbor.

    a. did
    b. had
    c. do

  3. A: Do people really ride horses in Mackinaw Island, Michigan?

    B: Yes, instead of cars they ___ ride horses.

    a. does
    b. do
    c. are

  4. Detroit, the biggest city in Michigan, is losing its importance. However, Chicago ___ theirs.

    a. increase
    b. is increasing
    c. isn't

  5. A: Can you recommend a good place to visit in Michigan?

    B: Have you ever been to South Haven? I ___ have good memories of a beautiful landscape there. Maybe you will get good memories in South Haven also.

    a. did
    b. do
    c. does

  6. Many hunters go to the Upper Peninsula for ___ hunting in Michigan.

    a. deers
    b. the deers
    c. deer

  7. Many thunderstorms are watched and many ___ of lightening are seen in Michigan.

    a. bolt
    b. flashes
    c. clap

  8. There is ___ major league baseball team in Michigan. It's the Detroit Tigers.

    a. a
    b. an
    c. the

  9. One of ___ is held in Detroit in January every year. It is called the Detroit International Automotive Car Show.

    a. the most famous car show
    b. a famous car show
    c. the most famous car shows

These questions were written by students who are enrolled in the advanced level grammar class in CELCIS which is located at Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, Michigan.
