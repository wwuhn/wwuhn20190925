Soccer in Brazil

    a.of

    d. on

  2. Soccer was first brought ___ Brazil ___ the British and Dutch sailors in the second half of the nineteenth century.
    a. to, for
    b. for, by
    c. to, by
    d. by, to

  3. Corinthians was ___ first British team that played in Brazil.
    a. a
    b. the
    c. an

  4. Nowadays Corinthians is one of ___ popular teams in Brazil.
    a. the most
    b. most
    c. more
    d. the more

  5. There's ___ doubt that soccer is the favorite sport in Brazil.

    b. no

    d. not

    b.had won

    b.who
    c.which
    d. whom

  9. Soccer coaching in Brazil usually begins ___ a very early age.
    a. in
    b.at
    c. on

  10. Even though there are many good soccer players in Brazil, Pele ___ as the greatest Brazilian soccer player.

    b.still recognized
    c.has still to recognize

  11. Some soccer teams include the name of the city or state ___ the team is based.
    a. which
    b.on which
    c.in where
    d.where

  12. The team scoring the greatest number of points at the end of the ___ season wins the Championship Cup.
    a. playing
    b.play
    c.played

  13. Soccer coaches ___ teams are unsuccessful for a number of consecutive games, are likely to lose their jobs.
    a. who's
    b.whose
    c. whom
    d. that

    a. wherever
    b.whenever
    c.whichever

  15. Of the four World Cup matches that Pele participated in, Brazil ___ three of them.
    a. wins
    b.had won
    c.won
