    a. scent

    c. cite

  18. It is not unusual to see a wild ___ in the hills near my house.
    a. boar
    b. bore
    c. boor

    a. buy
    b. by
    c. bye

  20. To remove the seeds from an apple one needs to cut out the ___.
    a. core
    b. corps
