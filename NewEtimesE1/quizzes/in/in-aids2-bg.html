  1. There are probably ___ million people in the world today (1999) with HIV/AIDS.
    a. 20
    b. 16
    c. 47
    d. 2.5

    a. 20,800,000
    b. 12,000,000
    c. 5,200,000
    d. 2,000,000

  3. During 1998, _______ children became infected with the HIV virus.
    a. 2,500,000
    b. 5,000,000
    c. 320,000
    d. 980,000

    a. 2,500,000
    b. 5,000,000
    c. 320,000
    d. 590,000

  5. In the whole world, there are about ______ children and young people (under 25) who become infected with HIV every day.
    a. 85
    b. 850
    c. 8,500
    d. 85,000

    b. 80,000
    c. 800,000
    d. 8,000,000

  7. About ______ of the people who became infected with the HIV virus in 1998 are women.
    a. one-fifth
    b. three-fifths
    c. one-half
    d. one-third

  8. About ninety percent of the children whose mothers have died of AIDS live in _______ .
    a. Asia
    b. the US
    c. Africa
    d. Europe

    a. US$10
    b. US$200
    c. US$2,000
    d. US$20,000

    a. US$10
