English USA Lesson 60, Part 2

1.

　

续Part 1。学习与人的所有物或持有物有关的表达法。马丁准备写一篇职业女性的报道，与一位妇女约了访谈时间，但是约定访谈的那位妇女所在的公司前一天晚上被小偷入室盗窃，办公室里物品凌乱。

L60P2_1.MP3[600KB]

This is English USA, on the Voice of America. Now, Lesson 60, Part 2.

JENNIFER:

This is not a good day.

MARTIN:

I can come back another day.

JENNIFER:

No, that's all right. We won't work much today. We'll have to clean up.

MARTIN:

Have you had a burglary before?

JENNIFER:

No, never. This is the first time.

MARTIN:

When did it happen?

JENNIFER:

During the night. The police don't know what time it happened.

MARTIN:

When did you leave yesterday?

JENNIFER:

I worked very late. I left the office about nine o'clock. I was the last person in the office. Look at this mess. These aren't my papers.

MARTIN:

Whose papers are they?

JENNIFER:

They're Rollin's. I think they're his. They look like his. He's working on a new book.

MARTIN:

Where is Rollin's office?

JENNIFER:

His office is on the other side.

MARTIN:

How did his papers get here?

JENNIFER:

Someone brought them. They weren't here last night.
MARTIN: Did the burglars bring them?

JENNIFER:

Why? And look at these! These are Phoebe's letters!

MARTIN:

How do you know?

JENNIFER:

They have her name on them.

MARTIN:

Were they here yesterday?

JENNIFER:

Of course not.

MARTIN:

Where is her office?

JENNIFER:

That one is hers.

MARTIN:

The one across the hall?

JENNIFER:

Yes. Her door is closed. She isn't here today. I don't understand any of this.

MARTIN:

I'm sure the police can help.

JENNIFER:

Why did the burglars make such a mess?

POLICEMAN:

Excuse me. May I come in?

JENNIFER:

Of course.

MARTIN:

Pardon me. I'll come another time.

POLICEMAN:

No, no. That's all right. I want to ask a few questions. I won't be a minute. What is missing from your office, Miss Donnelly?

JENNIFER:

I don't know. My computer.

POLICEMAN:

Are any of your papers missing?

JENNIFER:

I don't think so. My flowers! My flowers are missing!

POLICEMAN:

What kind of flowers?

JENNIFER:

Roses. Why does a burglar take flowers?

POLICEMAN:

Maybe for his wife. When did you leave the office yesterday afternoon?

JENNIFER:

It was night. My work wasn't finished. I worked late.

2.
　

形容词性代词所有格“my, your, her, his, our, their”+ 名词的表达法。请听马丁给出的例子。

MARTIN:

My home.

Your pen.

Her basket.

His papers.

3.

　

已经知道某物是什么，说某人的某物，可以用“mine, hers, his, yours, ours, theirs”这些名词性代词所有格来表达。请听马丁给出的例子。

L60P2_3.MP3[30KB]

MARTIN:

This is my home. It's mine.

That's your pen. It's yours.

This is her basket. It's hers.

Those are his papers. They're his.

My home. Mine.

Your pen. Yours.

Her basket. Hers.

His papers. His.

4.


　

下面马丁问你“这是某某人的东西吗？”以及这是谁的东西。你用“是的，这是某某的东西。”以及 “mine, hers, his, yours, ours, theirs”这些名词性代词所有格来肯定回答问题。之后是前段对话的继续。

L60P2_4.MP3[271KB]

MARTIN:

Is that her basket?

YOU:

[Yes, it's hers.]

MARTIN:

Is that his pen?

YOU:

[Yes, it's his.]

MARTIN:

Are those her papers?

YOU:

[Yes, they are hers.]

POLICEMAN:

Has anything else been taken?

JENNIFER:

I don't know. I don't think so.

POLICEMAN:

OK. I'll come back in a few minutes.

JENNIFER:

All right. I'm sorry, Mr. Learner.

MARTIN:

That's all right.

JENNIFER:

What did you want to talk about?

MARTIN:

Your work. I'm writing a story about women in business.

JENNIFER:

I'm sorry about the problems today. We've never had a burglary before.

MARTIN:

That's all right. It has been interesting. May I come back after lunch?

JENNIFER:

That would be fine. The police will go soon. I can talk better this afternoon. Thank you for understanding.

MARTIN:

No problem. I'll see you after lunch.
