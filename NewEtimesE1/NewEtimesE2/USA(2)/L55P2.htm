English USA Lesson 55, Part 2

1.

　

续Part 1。学习向初次见面的人们询问姓名以及报上自己的姓名。另外在本节还学习“It's late, isn't it?”的附加疑问句句型。马丁到只在暑假期间上演剧目的“夏季剧院”剧院厅采访。

L55P2_1.MP3[521KB]

This is English USA, on the Voice of America. Now, Lesson 55, Part 2.

JOHN:

This is the backstage area.

MARTIN:

It's very small, isn't it?

JOHN:

Yes. We have a large stage. We can use a lot of actors. But the backstage area is too small. We can't move.

MARTIN:

What do you do?

JOHN:

Come and see. This is the men's dressing room. We have 12 men and boys in the play.

JOHN:

How are you doing, everybody? This is Martin Learner.

MARTIN:

Thanks for letting me visit. How many minutes until the play begins?

JOHN:

Thirty minutes.

MARTIN:

You're Macbeth, aren't you?

PAUL:

Right.

MARTIN:

You look different now.

PAUL:

Good. Do I look like Macbeth?

MARTIN:

Yes, you do.

PAUL:

Last week I was a clown. In Twelfth Night.

MARTIN:

How many plays are you in during the summer?

PAUL:

We do three plays. We are in all of them. I have one big part. That's Macbeth, of course. I have small parts in the other plays.

MARTIN:

This is the first night for Macbeth, isn't it? Are you afraid?

PAUL:

A little.

2.
　

附加疑问句句型在征求对方确认或者同时使用。请听录音，对照前一课与本课的用法。

L55P2_2.MP3[76KB]

MARTIN:

It's crowded, isn't it?

It's late, isn't it?

It's very small, isn't it?

The stage is large, isn't it?

It's crowded on the weekend, isn't it?

This is the first night for Macbeth, isn't it?

3.




　

附加疑问句句型在会话中经常使用。附加疑问句部分不必与前面的陈述句部分对应。例如，“It is crowded, isn't it?” 前面的陈述句部分是肯定形式的，后面的附加疑问句就要用否定形式。另一方面，“He is your director, isn't he?”的附加疑问句部分用的代词是跟前面陈述句的代词相呼应的。下一课继续学习附加疑问句。请再听一遍本课出现的附加疑问句。

L55P2_3.MP3[62KB]

MARTIN:

The stage is large, isn't it?

He's your director, isn't he?

You're Macbeth, aren't you?

Isn't it? Isn't he? Aren't you?

4.

以下是前段对话的继续。

MARTIN:

How are you doing?

BETH:

Very well, thank you.

MARTIN:

Are you afraid?

BETH:

She's afraid. Wendy, this is Martin Learner. Wendy is playing Lady Macduff. It is her first time on the stage.

MARTIN:

And what's her name?

BETH:

Her name is Moon.

MARTIN:

Pardon me.

BETH:

Moon. You know, like moon, stars, sun. Moon.

MARTIN:

Wonderful.

BETH:

Excuse me, Martin. I must finish.

MARTIN:

Thanks. I'm going to talk with the other women.
