English USA Lesson 49, Part 1

1.
　

学习表达想要什么东西或需要什么东西。马丁要代替爱莲到超市去购物，于是跟家人谈起有关购物的事情。

L49P1_1.MP3[616KB]

This is English USA, on the Voice of America. Now, Lesson 49, Part 1.

EILEEN:

Martin, turn the radio down please.

EILEEN:

Are you going to have a busy day, Martin?

MARTIN:

Not too busy. I'm going to finish a story.

EILEEN:

Sue, please call Alan. His breakfast is getting cold.

MARTIN:

What are you doing today?

SUE:

Alan! Hurry up! You're late.

EILEEN:

I'm going to be very busy today.

MARTIN:

Do you want me to shop today?

EILEEN:

Can you? I can't come home until seven o'clock. Can you shop?

MARTIN:

Sure.

ALAN:

Good morning.

EILEEN:

Good morning.

*1

MARTIN:

Morning, Alan.

ALAN:

I didn't sleep last night.

EILEEN:

Are you sick?

ALAN:

I don't think so. May I have some tea?

EILEEN:

Eat something too.

MARTIN:

I'm going shopping today. What do you need?

SUE:

Me? I need things for school. I need some notebooks. And I want a new pen. I don't like my pen.

MARTIN:

I'm going to buy food. What food do you want?

ALAN:

I don't want any food.

MARTIN:

You are sick.

SUE:

He listened to his stereo all night.

ALAN:

I did not.

SUE:

I heard it. All night.

EILEEN:

Now, now. Let's help daddy. What do we need from the store?

MARTIN:

What do you want, Eileen.

EILEEN:

Please buy some nice tomatoes.

MARTIN:

OK. Anything else?

EILEEN:

Buy some onions.

MARTIN:

We need some milk.

EILEEN:

And bread.

SUE:

I want some new cereal. I don't like this cereal.

EILEEN:

You wanted it.

SUE:

I don't like it.

EILEEN:

What do you want?

SUE:

I don't know. Daddy, you buy some cereal.

MARTIN:

Do we need any eggs?

ALAN:

Ugh. Don't talk about eggs.

SUE:

Yes, he's sick.

EILEEN:

I want some green vegetables too.

MARTIN:

Do we need any meat?

EILEEN:

No, we have some. Buy some fruit.

MARTIN:

Sue, what kind of fruit do you want?

SUE:

Bananas.

MARTIN:

Alan, what kind of fruit do you want?

ALAN:

Oranges. Do we have any oranges now?

EILEEN:

Yes. Do you want one?

ALAN:

Please.

3.
　

马丁问你想要什么，你根据自己的实际回答问题。然后是前一段对话的继续。

L49P1_3.MP3[277KB]

MARTIN:

Do you want any vegetables?

YOU:

[Yes, I want ----.] or [No, I don't want any vegetables.]

MARTIN:

Do you want some fruit?

YOU:

[Yes, I want ----.] or [No, I don't want any fruit.]

MARTIN:

What kind of fruit do you want?

YOU:

[I want ----.]

MARTIN:

What kind of vegetables do you want?

YOU:

[I want ----.]

EILEEN:

Alan, you look terrible.

MARTIN:

You look sick.

EILEEN:

Let me feel your head. You are sick. You're too hot. Do you
want to go to the doctor?

Not today. Can I stay home?

EILEEN:

Of course.

Can I stay home?

EILEEN:

Of course not.
