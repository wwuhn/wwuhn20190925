English USA Lesson 45, Part 2
　

1.
　

续Part 1。学习描述物体形状和功能。马丁访问机器人制造厂，正在工场里。

L45P2_1.MP3[729KB]

　 This is English USA, on the Voice of America. Now, Lesson 45 Part 2.
　 　

　

MARTIN:

These robots are very interesting, Bernice. Do any robots talk?

　

BERNICE:

This one is voice activated and it talks.

　

MARTIN:

What does voice activated mean?

　

BERNICE:

You can talk to it.

　

MARTIN:

What can I talk about?

　

BERNICE:

You can tell it the work to do. Look.  Charlie. Clean forward.

　

BERNICE:

Charlie. Clean backward.

　

BERNICE:

Tell it to stop.

　

MARTIN:

Stop.

　

BERNICE:

Speak firmly.

　

MARTIN:

Charlie! Stop!

　

MARTIN:

That's amazing. Wonderful.

　

BERNICE:

I love Charlie.

　

MARTIN:

Why do you call the robot Charlie?

　

BERNICE:

Charlie is my husband's name.

　

MARTIN:

I see.

　

BERNICE:

Charlie speaks three sentences. Listen.

　

CHARLIE:

Turn me off. Turn me off. Turn me off.

　

MARTIN:

What else does he say?

　

BERNICE:

Listen.

　

CHARLIE:

I am empty. I am empty. I am empty. I am --

　

BERNICE:

That's enough, Charlie. Don't you love Charlie?

　

MARTIN:

He's fascinating. Are there more robots?

*1

BERNICE:

Yes. We make six robots. Come see the other robots.

　

　

　

　

BERNICE:

Martin, this is Sandy Costner. He works on this robot.

　

MARTIN:

Hi. What does this robot do?

　

SANDY:

She folds boxes.

　

MARTIN:

She? Why she?

　

SANDY:

She's a good worker. She's smart. She's not very big.

　

MARTIN:

What is her name?

　

SANDY:

Name? She doesn't have a name.

　

MARTIN:

I met Charlie a few minutes ago.

　

SANDY:

I see.

　

BERNICE:

She's not very intelligent. She's simple. She moves this way. Then she moves that way. That's it. But look at this robot.

　

SANDY:

This robot is new.

　

MARTIN:

What does it do?

　

SANDY:

It paints.

　

BERNICE:

It has six arms.

　

MARTIN:

And it has fingers.

　

SANDY:

It paints the outside. Then it paints the inside. Then it puts a label outside.

　

MARTIN:

What does it paint?

　

SANDY:

Anything. It can paint square things, or round things. It can paint small things, or big things.

　

MARTIN:

That's amazing.

*1

Come see the other robots.

Come see 等于 Come and see 或者Come to see

　

2.
　

形容词用于所修饰的名词前。请听马丁先说名词，然后把形容词及其所修饰的名词和在一起说。

L45P2_2.MP3[75KB]

　 MARTIN:

Workers.    Better workers.    Hands.    Small hands.

Holes.    Tiny holes.    Robot.    Next robot.

Worker.    Good worker.    Things.    Square things.

　

3.
　

"be"动词后面跟形容~。这时形容词描述作主语的名词或代词。请听以下代词+"be"动词+形容词的例句。

L45P2_3.MP3[67KB]

　 MARTIN:

It's hot.    It's heavy.    It's intelligent.    It's not tall.

You're taller.    They're long.    That's amazing.

He's fascinating.
