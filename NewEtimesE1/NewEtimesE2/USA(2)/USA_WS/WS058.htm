Hi. This is Ray McDonald and Nancy Beardsley with words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 58. We begin with these nouns:

copy

拷贝，副本

traffic

交通

parking lot

停车场

Next, some verbs:

apologize

道歉

pour

泼，洒

forget / forgot

忘记/forget的过去式

rain

下雨
It's raining very hard.

hurt

使受伤

sign

签名

leave / left

离开/leave的过去分词

worry

担心，忧虑

These are adjectives:

early

 及早的，早先的

successful

成功的
late 迟到的
terrible 可怕的

Here are some phrases:

all morning / all night / all day

整个早上/整个晚上/一整天

all right

没关系
That's all right.

No problem. / There is no problem.

没问题。/没问题。

Finally, some grammar notes - examples of the present perfect tense:

I have just arrived.

我刚到。

You have come about NEAT. (Necessary Environmental Action Today)

你是为NEAT的事情来的。

I have spilled coffee.

我把咖啡弄洒了。

The rain has been terrible.

这场雨很可怕。

It has not been a good day.

这一天过得很差劲。
