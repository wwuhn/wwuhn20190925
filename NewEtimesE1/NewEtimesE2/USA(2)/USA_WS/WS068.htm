Hi. This is Ray McDonald and Nancy Beardsley with words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 68. We begin with a list of nouns:

accident

elevator

game

游戏，赛事

machine

message

These are verbs:

operate

操作，运行（机器）

relax

run

Here is an adjective:

electrical

电力的，电的
electrical fires: 电火花

Finally, this grammar note:

Two sentences about the same subject can be combined. Examples in this lesson include these types: [两个句子谈及同一个主语时可以合成为一个句子。以下是本课出现的一些例子。]

He's the one (=person).
He runs the store.

就是他。

他经营这家商店。

He's the one who runs the store.

他是经营这家商店的人。

The man was very young.
He was playing that game.

那男的非常年轻。

他正在玩游戏。

The man who was playing that game was very young.

正在玩游戏的那个男子非常年轻。
