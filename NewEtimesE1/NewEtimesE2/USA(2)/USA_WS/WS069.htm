Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 69. We begin with these nouns:

attitude

态度，看法

literature

文学，文献

calendar

日历

Native American

美国土著，本土美国人

celebration

庆祝，庆典

powwow

帕瓦仪式

craft

工艺品，手工艺品

religion

宗教

customs [custom]

风俗，习俗

story telling

讲故事

date

日期

term

术语，用语

discussion

讨论

topic

话题

history

历史

Here are some verbs:

check

检查，核对
I'm checking the calendar.

participate

参加
Can anyone participate the meeting?

share

分享，共享
We come together and share our culture.

Next are some adjectives:

educational

教育的
educational activities

modern

现代的
modern Indian

possible

可能的
Is it possible for me to attend powwow?

Finally, this phrase:
The sooner the better. 越快越好。
