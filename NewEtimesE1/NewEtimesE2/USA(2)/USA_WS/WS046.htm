Hi. This is Ray McDonald and Nancy Beardsley with words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 46. We begin with these nouns that are places in Washington:

Capitol Building

国会大厦

Capitol Hill

美国国会山，美国国会

Library of Congress

美国国会图书馆

Lincoln Memorial

林肯纪念堂

Mall

[地名]莫尔

Union Station

联合车站

White House

白宫

Here are some other nouns:

area

范围，区域，面积

Congress /congressman / congresswoman

国会/国会议员（男）/国会议员（女）

food

食物，食品

hill

山岗，小峁

neighborhood

近邻，邻居

my neighborhood: 我的邻居们

stadium

体育馆

government

政府

Next is a list of adjectives:

low

矮的，低的
We are on a low hill.

private

私人的
private homes

rich

富裕的

several

一些，几个

wealthy

富裕的，有钱的

Finally, these two verbs:

renovate

 刷新，修复

sleep

 睡觉
