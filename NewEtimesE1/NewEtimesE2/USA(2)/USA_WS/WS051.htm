Finally, some grammar notes:
Many comparatives are formed by adding -er - or -est to adjectives.
This radio is small. 这台收音机很小。
This radio is smaller. 这台收音机更小。
This radio is the smallest. 这台收音机最小。
Other comparatives add the words "more" or "most" instead of -er or -est.
This radio is expensive. 这台收音机很贵。
This radio is more expensive. 这台收音机根贵。
This radio is the most expensive. 这台收音机最贵。
