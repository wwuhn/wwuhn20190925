half a pound 半磅

And these cultural notes:

Items in a grocery store or at a market are priced by different measures and quantities. Here are some common ones:

Weight is measured in pounds and ounces.
Containers may be a box, a bag, a can, a jar or a bottle.
Amount may be a bunch or a piece.
Number may be ten or a dozen.
