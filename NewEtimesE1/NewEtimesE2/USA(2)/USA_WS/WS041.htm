Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 41. First, these nouns: （名词）

　

clinic

诊疗所

food

食物

　

influenza (= flu)

流感、感冒

leg

脚

　

mathematics

数学

　

patient

患者，病人

　

photography

撮影(术)

　

problem

问题、难题

　

shots

注射 [a shot]

We come here for our shots.

　

Here are some adjectives: (形容词）

　

broken

骨折

His leg is broken now.

　

busy

忙

　

healthy

健康

　

helpful

有帮助的，有用的

　

old

年老的，旧的

　

poor

贫穷的

　

sick

有病的，恶心的

　

smart

聪明的

　

strong

（身体）强壮的

　

tired

疲劳的

I am tired.

　

weak

弱的

　

well

（身体）健康的，康复的

She is getting well.

　

young

年轻的

　

Next, two tenses of a verb: （动词）

　

break / broke

(骨)折断

He broke his leg.

　

Finally, these adverbs: （副词）

　

hard

刻苦地

She works very hard.

　

mostly

大部分

This neighborhood is mostly old people.
