 Words & Phrases for Lesson 55

Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 55. First are these nouns:

audience

听众

musical

音乐喜剧

theater

剧院

lodging

（出租的）房间

cost

开销

scene

场景

auditorium

听众席，观众席

costumes

服装，装束

expenses

费用，开支

salary

工资

stage

舞台

These are verbs:

base

基于...；以...为基础

It's based on Shakespeare.

interrupt

打断，中止

bother

烦扰，打扰

direct

指引，指挥，导演

play

扮演，播放，玩

frighten

使惊吓

worry

使担忧

perform

表演，演出，执行，履行

Here are two adjectives:

perfect

完美的
His play was perfect.

ready

准备好了的
I feel ready.

And two adverbs:

backstage

后台

Come backstage.

never

从不，决不
It's never a good time to talk.

Next, an idiom and another phrase:

make money

赚钱
We don't make any money in the summer.

look like

看上去像
Do I look like Macbeth?

Finally, some questions and answers:
The stage is large, isn't it? 舞台很大，是吗？
Yes, it is. 是的，很大。
Martin is a reporter, isn't he? 马丁是记者，对吧？
Yes, he is. 是的，他是记者。
He's the manager, isn't he? 他是经理，对吗？
No, he isn't. 不对，他不是。
Beth is playing Lady Macduff, isn't she? 贝丝扮演Macduff夫人，是不是？
No, she isn't. 不是。
