Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 65. We begin with these nouns:

business

生意，买卖，交易

ocean

大洋

fishing

捕鱼

fisherman / fishermen

渔民 / fisherman的复数形式

line

绳索，航线，线路

money

钱

net

网

profit

利润

tank

箱，柜

trawler

拖捞传

wholesaler

批发商

These are verbs:
catch 捕捉
fish 鱼
hate 憎恨，厌恶
storm 下起暴风雨，降暴风雪
troll 钓鱼

Next, two adjectives:
cone shaped 锥形的
special 特别的

Here is an adverb:
farther

副词far（远处）的比较级
far - farther - farthest

Finally, these phrases:
I hope so. 我希望如此。
At last. 终于/到底（完成了，到了）。
