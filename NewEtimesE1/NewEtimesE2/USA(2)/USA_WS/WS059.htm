Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 59. We begin with these nouns:

activity

活动
[physical activity 身体活动]

class

班级

classroom

教室

group

组，集团

milk

牛奶

playground

运动场

skill

技能

sugar

糖

Here are some verbs:

agree

同意
I agree with you.

grow / grown

生长，成长/grow的过去分词
(过去式是grew)
The neighborhood has grown very quickly.

These are adjectives:

academic

学术的，学院的
[academic skill：学术技能]

confused

困惑的，烦恼的

crowded

拥挤的

difficult

困难的

poor

贫穷的

rich

富裕的

social

社会的
[social skill：社会技能]

Finally, two adverbs:

privately

私下地

quickly

迅速地
