English USA Translation
Lesson 57, Part 2

L57P2J.MP3[652KB]

MARTIN:

Excuse me, I'm Martin Learner. May I ask you some questions?

打搅了，我叫马丁・雷纳。可以请教你几个问题吗？

SANDY:

What for?

为什么？

MARTIN:

I have a petition I want you to read.

我有一份请愿书想让你读读。

SANDY:

What for?

为什么？

MARTIN:

It's about the park. We want to get the park cleaned up.

是关于公园的。我们想把公园彻底弄干净。

JAN:

This park?

这座公园吗？

MARTIN:

Yes. Do you live in the neighborhood?

是的。你住在这个社区吗？

JAN:

Yes. We both live here.

是的。我们俩都住这。

MARTIN:

Please read the petition.

请读读这份请愿书。

SANDY:

Let me see it.

让我看看。

MARTIN:

Where do you live?

你住在哪里？

JAN:

I live at---

我住在----

SANDY:

Wait a minute. I want to read this first. Do you know this man?

请等等。我想先读读这个。你认识这个人吗？

JAN:

Yes, I do. We go on the same train to Washington sometimes.

是的，我认识。我们有时候搭乘同一趟火车去华盛顿。

SANDY:

OK. Go ahead. I like the petition.

好。往下干。我喜欢这份请愿书。

MARTIN:

Will you sign it?

你愿意在上面签名吗？

SANDY:

Sure. Here.

当然。在这。

MARTIN:

I need to ask some more questions.

我需要多问几个问题？

SANDY:

OK.

好的。

MARTIN:

How old are you?

请问你的年龄？

SANDY:

I'm 25 years old.

25岁。

MARTIN:

Where do you live?

你住在哪里？

SANDY:

I live at 502 Hillside Avenue. That's up the hill.

我住在希尔塞得大街502号。就是在那山岗上。

MARTIN:

Yes, I know. I live up that way too. How long have you lived in the neighborhood？

我知道。我也住在那一带。你在这个社区住了多久了？

SANDY:

Always. I was born here. My parents lived here.

一直住在这里。我是在这里出生的。我父母原来也住在这里。

JAN:

They still live here.

他们现在仍然住在这里。

MARTIN:

What's your name?

请问尊姓大名？

SANDY:

Sandy Welch.

桑迪・威尔赤。

MARTIN:

And what's your name?

请问你的尊姓大名？

JAN:

I'm Jan Whiting.

珍・怀婷。

MARTIN:

Are you going to sign?

你打算签名吗？

JAN:

Yes, I like the petition.

是的，我喜欢这份请愿书。

MARTIN:

Where do you live, Jan?

你在哪里住，珍？

JAN:

I live on the other side of the park.

我住在公园的那一边。

MARTIN:

What's your address?

请问你的地址？

JAN:

941 Elmore Lane. My house is that yellow one over there.

埃默尔路941号。我的房子就是那边黄色的那间。

MARTIN:

When were you born?

你何时出生？

JAN:

In 1969.

1969年。

MARTIN:

How long have you lived in the neighborhood?

你在这个社区住了多久？

JAN:

I came here in 1979.

我1979年来这里。

MARTIN:

Where did you live before? Did you live in Baltimore?

在那之前你住在哪里？你在巴尔迪莫住过吗？

JAN:

Yes, but not in this neighborhood. I lived on the far west side of the city.

是的，不过不在这个社区。我当时住在偏远的城西。

MARTIN:

Did you sign, Sandy?

你签名了吗，桑迪？

SANDY:

Not yet. Here, I'll sign now.

还没签。在这。我现在就签。

MARTIN:

Thanks. Now you, Jan.

谢谢。现在轮到你了，珍。

JAN:

I'm very happy to sign. The park is dangerous for children. It's not very clean.

我很乐意签名。这座公园对孩子们来说很危险。公园不干净。

SANDY:

There's a lot of trash on weekends. Glass bottles and paper.

周末很多垃圾。玻璃瓶子和报纸。

MARTIN:

Thanks for helping.

谢谢帮忙。

JAN:

Is there something we can do?

还有什么事情是我们能够做的？

MARTIN:

You can come to our meetings. We meet on Thursday evenings at the Catholic church.

你可以明天来开会。我们在天主教堂风周四晚上开会。

SANDY:

What's the address?

请问地址？

MARTIN:

510 Saint Barbara Street.

圣・巴巴拉大街510号。

MARTIN:

Hello. How are you doing?

你好。感觉怎么样？

CYNTHIA:

Fine. I have several names.

很好。我找到几个人签名了。

MARTIN:

That's good. I have a few.

好哇。我也找到几个人签名了。

CYNTHIA:

Would you like some coffee? I brought some.

你要来点咖啡吗？我带了些来。

MARTIN:

That sounds wonderful.

好哇。

MARTIN:

Where do you live, Cynthia?

你在哪里住，辛西娅？

CYNTHIA:

On Parker Drive. 260 Parker Drive. It's near my school.

在派克快车道。派克快车道260号。在我学校附近。

MARTIN:

Of course. I know where it is.

没问题。我知道在哪里。

CYNTHIA:

I'm going to the other side of the park again.

我还要去公园的那一边。

MARTIN:

Thanks for the coffee.

谢谢你的咖啡。

CYNTHIA:

Don't mention it.

别客气。

MARTIN:

Harvey Mountain and his wife are coming at 11 o'clock.

哈维・毛恩田和他的太太在十一点过来。

CYNTHIA:

Good. Send one of them to me.

好。让他们中的一个来见我。

MARTIN:

Bye.

再见。
