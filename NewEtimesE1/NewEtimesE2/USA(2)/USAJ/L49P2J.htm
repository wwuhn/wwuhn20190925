English USA Translation
Lesson 49, Part 2

L49P2J.MP3[502KB]

MARTIN:

Sue, are you ready for school?

苏，你准备好可以上学了吗？

SUE:

Yes.

好了。

EILEEN:

Please finish your breakfast. We need to go.

请吃完早餐，我们要走了。

SUE:

Are you taking me today, mama?

你带我去吗，妈妈？

EILEEN:

Yes. We need to go in five minutes.

是的。我们五分钟后就走。

SUE:

OK. Do I have to eat this?

好的。我要吃这个吗？

EILEEN:

Yes, please. It's good for you.

没错。对你有益。

MARTIN:

Alan, does your head ache?

阿兰，你头疼吗？

ALAN:

No. I'm tired.

不头疼。我只是累。

MARTIN:

Do you need anything from the store?

你要从商店给你买点什么东西吗？

ALAN:

No. Yes. Something to drink.

不用了。哦，来点喝的吧。

MARTIN:

What do you want?

想要什么？

ALAN:

Lemon soda.

柠檬苏打水。

MARTIN:

Right. Eileen, anything else?

好的。爱莲，还有别的吗？

EILEEN:

Did you write vegetables?

你把蔬菜记下来了吗？

MARTIN:

Yes, I did. What kind of vegetables do you want?

记下来了。你想要哪种蔬菜？

EILEEN:

Green vegetables. Green beans. Peas. Broccoli. Any green vegetable.

绿色的蔬菜。青豆，豌豆，椰菜。但凡是绿色的蔬菜。

MARTIN:

Do you want something for salad?

你要买点什么做色拉吗？

EILEEN:

Did you write tomatoes?

你记下西红柿了吗？

MARTIN:

Yes.

是的。

EILEEN:

Do you want lettuce?

你要生菜吗？

MARTIN:

It's OK.

好的。

EILEEN:

Look at the store. Find something interesting.

在商店里好好看看，找一些有意思的东西。

SUE:

Do I have to eat salad?

我要吃色拉吗？

EILEEN:

Don't talk about salad now. Finish your breakfast.

现在别说色拉。先吃完你的早餐。

MARTIN:

Do we need coffee?

我们需要咖啡吗？

EILEEN:

Oh, yes! And we need sugar.

啊，是的。而且我们还需要糖。

MARTIN:

Do we need tea?

我们需要茶叶吗？

EILEEN:

Not today. We have some.

今天不需要。我们还有一些。

MARTIN:

Do we need flour?

我们需要面粉吗？

EILEEN:

I don't know. Please look.

不知道。请看看。

MARTIN:

Yes, we need flour. Do you want brown or white flour?

是的，我们需要面粉。你想要黑面还是白面？

EILEEN:

Both.

都要。

SUE:

I need some cookies. The cookies are finished.

我需要一些曲奇饼。曲奇饼已经吃完了。

EILEEN:

Finished?!

吃完了？

SUE:

Alan ate them.

阿兰吃掉了。

ALAN:

No, I didn't.

没有，我没吃。

EILEEN:

How many did you eat yesterday?

你昨天吃了多少？

SUE:

A few.

几块。

EILEEN:

How many?

多少？

SUE:

Some.

好几块。

EILEEN:

How many?

多少？

SUE:

Five. Alan ate a lot.

五块。阿兰吃了很多。

EILEEN:

Alan, how many did you eat?

阿兰，你吃了多少？

ALAN:

I don't know. A lot.

我不知道。反正很多。

MARTIN:

That's too many cookies. No more cookies this week. What else do we need?

这下吃了太多曲奇饼了。这周没有曲奇饼吃了。我们还需要什么？

ALAN:

May I have some soup?

我可以喝些汤吗？

EILEEN:

Of course. We have some. What kind do you want?

当然可以。我们有汤。你要那种汤？

ALAN:

Tomato.

土豆汤。

MARTIN:

OK. Tomato soup. Anything else? What do you want for dinner tonight?

好的。土豆汤。还有别的吗？今天晚上你想吃什么晚餐？

SUE:

Can we have pizza?

我们可以吃比萨饼吗？

EILEEN:

That's a good idea.

好主意。

MARTIN:

Do we have everything we need for pizza?

要吃比萨比，我们把东西配齐了吗？

EILEEN:

Sure.

当然了。

EILEEN:

Sue. Let's go. I'm going to be late. Alan, please go to bed. Goodbye, Martin.

苏，咱们走吧。我就要迟到了。阿兰，你上床躺着。再见，马丁。

MARTIN:

Goodbye. See you tonight.

再见。晚上见。
