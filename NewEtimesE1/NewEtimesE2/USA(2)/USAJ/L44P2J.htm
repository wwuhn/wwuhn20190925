English USA Translation
　

L44P2J.MP3[647KB]

　

MARTIN:

Do you have a family?

　

　

你成家了吗？

　

DRIVER:

I'm divorced.

　

　

我离婚了。

　

MARTIN:

Do you have any children?

　

　

你有孩子吗？

　

DRIVER:

I have a boy. He lives with his mother. I visit him. We go interesting places.

　

　

我有一个男孩。他跟他妈妈住。我会去看他。我们会去一些好玩的地方。

　

MARTIN:

What places?

　

　

什么地方？

　

DRIVER:

He likes movies. We go to ball games. He likes camping and fishing.

　

　

他喜欢看电影。我们去玩球类活动。他喜欢露营和钓鱼。

　

MARTIN:

How old is he?

　

　

他多大了？

　

DRIVER:

He's twelve years old.

　

　

12岁。

　

MARTIN:

Take him to Baltimore. He's going to like the educational places.

　

　

带他去巴的摩亚吧。他会喜欢上那里的地方，很有教育意义的。

　

DRIVER:

What can we do?

　

　

我们在那能干什么？

　

MARTIN:

You can see a wonderful aquarium. You can visit some very old ships in the harbor. You can take your son to an excellent railroad museum.

　

　

你可以去参观漂亮的水族馆。可以在港口看古董船。你可以带上你的儿子去参观火车博物馆，那里很好的。

　

DRIVER:

He loves trains.

　

　

他喜欢火车。

　

MARTIN:

Baltimore has an art museum, theaters, and a huge convention building.

　

　

巴尔迪摩有一座美术博物馆，戏院和一座很大的大会大楼。

　

DRIVER:

And we can go to a ball game.

　

　

我们能去打一下球。

　

MARTIN:

Yes. It has a new baseball park. It's beautiful. Go in the summer. It's warm and sunny.

　

　

对，那有一个新的棒球场。很漂亮。夏天去吧。天气晴朗又暖和。

　

DRIVER:

Can we go at night?

　

　

我们能够晚上去吗？

　

MARTIN:

Of course. I love baseball at night.

　

　

当然可以。我喜欢晚上打棒球。

　

DRIVER:

How much does it cost? I don't have much money.

　

　

要花多少钱？我钱不多。

　

MARTIN:

It's not expensive. You can fly. But you can go by train, or bus, or car too. Do you have a car?

　

　

花费并不高。你可以坐飞机。但是你也可以坐火车、汽车或者小车。你有小车吗？

　

DRIVER:

I have an old van. How long does it take to drive?

　

　

我有一辆旧货车。去那要开多久的车？

　

MARTIN:

About fifteen or sixteen hours.

　

　

大概十五到十六个小时吧。

　

DRIVER:

We can camp.

　

　

我们可以露营。

　

MARTIN:

Pardon?

　

　

什么？

　

DRIVER:

We can camp one night.

　

　

我们可以露营一个晚上。

　

MARTIN:

That's an excellent idea. You can camp in West Virginia. That's about five or six hours from Baltimore. You can drive one day. Then camp one night in West Virginia. Then drive to Baltimore the next day.

　

　

那真是个好主意。你可以在西维珍吉亚露营。大概离巴的摩亚有五到六个小时的车程吧。你可以开一天的车。然后在西维珍吉亚露营。然后第二天开车到巴尔的摩。

　

DRIVER:

Can we camp near Baltimore?

　

　

我们能在巴尔的摩露营吗？

　

MARTIN:

Certainly. You can find several camps outside the city.

　

　

当然可以。你可以在市郊区找到几个露营点。

　

DRIVER:

Are they expensive?

　

　

要花很多钱吗？

　

MARTIN:

I don't know. They are very popular.

　

　

我不清楚。但那些地方都很受欢迎。

　

　

　

　

DRIVER:

Do you travel often?

　

　

你经常旅游吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I do.

　

　

是的。我经常旅游。

　

DRIVER:

Is it interesting?

　

　

很有趣吗？

　

MARTIN:

Sometimes. I see exciting places and meet colorful people. Do you like your work?

　

　

有时候是。我去到让人激动的地方，碰到各色人等。你喜欢你的工作吗？

　

DRIVER:

I meet colorful people too. I have a second job.

　

　

我也碰到各种各样的人。我还有一份工作。

　

MARTIN:

What is that job?

　

　

什么工作？

　

DRIVER:

I work in a small bakery. I work there from 3 o'clock to 8 o'clock.

　

　

我在一家小面包店里工作。我在那从三点干到八点。

　

MARTIN:

In the morning?

　

　

早上？

　

DRIVER:

Yes. Then I drive this taxi.

　

　

对。然后我就开这出租车。

　

MARTIN:

Isn't that difficult?

　

　

很难吗？

　

DRIVER:

The bakery is hard work. The driving is easy. I like both jobs. I need the extra money.

　

　

面包店那份工作很辛苦，但开车就容易了。我两份工作都喜欢。我需要赚外快。

　

DRIVER:

Do you like Southern California?

　

　

你喜欢南加利福尼亚吗？

　

MARTIN:

Everyone likes Southern California. But I like Northern California too. Southern California is crowded. It's very lively. It's exciting.

　

　

所有人都喜欢南加利福尼亚。但我也喜欢北加利福尼亚。南加利福尼亚很多人。很有生机，很让人激动。

　

DRIVER:

Can I find work?

　

　

我能打到工作吗？

　

MARTIN:

Maybe. I don't know.

　

　

可能吧，我不知道。

　

DRIVER:

I need enough money. I want to look for good work.

　

　

我需要足够的钱。我想找份好工作。

　

MARTIN:

That's right. Save enough money.

　

　

对。存好钱。

　

DRIVER:

I can go in two or three years.

　

　

那我就能在两三年后去了。
