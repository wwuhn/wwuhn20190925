English USA Translation
Lesson 55, Part 1

L55P1J.MP3[646KB]

[The Thane of Fife* had a wife. Where is she now? What, will these hands---]

[法夫的大领主有过一位太太。如今她在何方？哦，这些帮工们...]

MARTIN:

Hello.

你好。

BETH:

Ahhh. You frightened me! Good afternoon.

啊，你吓着我了。早上好。

MARTIN:

Lady Macbeth. I should be frightened.

马克白夫人。应当是你吓着我了。

BETH:

Not in this scene. I'm still practicing my big scenes. We open tonight.

不是在这一场戏。我仍然在排练我演主角的几场戏。我们今晚上演。

MARTIN:

Don't let me interrupt. I'm looking for John Keasy. He's your director, isn't he?

不要让我打断你。我在找约翰・凯西。他是导演，是吗？

BETH:

Yes. He's in the theater. I saw him a few minutes ago.

是的，他在剧院里。几分钟前我还看见他。

MARTIN:

I won't bother you now, but may I ask you some questions later?

我不想现在打扰你。不过，待会儿我可以问你几个问题吗？

BETH:

Of course. Come backstage. We can talk there.

当然可以。到后台吧。我们可以在那谈谈。

MARTIN:

Thanks.

谢谢。

*Fife: 法夫

A region of eastern Scotland between the Firths of Forth and Tay. It was once a Pict kingdom. 苏格兰东部一地区，位于福斯湾和泰湾之间。曾是皮科特王国的土地

MARTIN:

Good afternoon. I'm Martin Learner. I'm looking for the director, John Keasy.

下午好。我是马丁・雷纳。我在找导演约翰・凯西。

JOHN:

That's me. I'm John Keasy. You're the reporter?

我就是。我是约翰・凯西。你是那位记者？

MARTIN:

That's right. I'm Martin Learner. Is this a good time to talk?

是的。我是马丁・雷纳。现在访谈时间合适吗？

JOHN:

It's never a good time to talk.

从来就没有合适的时间访谈。

MARTIN:

Are you worried?

你有什么烦恼吗？

JOHN:

Yes, of course.

当然有。

MARTIN:

Why?

为什么？

JOHN:

I never feel ready. It's never good enough. We need more time. The lights aren't right. The sound isn't good. The actors aren't loud enough. The audience won't come. The actors will get sick. The audience won't like it.

我总是觉得没准备好。总是不够好。我们需要更多的时间。灯光不对劲，声音效果不好，演员们声音不够大，观众不来看戏。演员们生病。观众不喜欢这出戏。

MARTIN:

Will it be bad?

这出戏会很蹩脚吗?

JOHN:

No. It never is. It won't be perfect, but it won't be bad. But it's OK. I have time to talk now.

不，绝对不会。虽然算不上十全十美，但决不会蹩脚的。至少还过得去。我现在有时间跟你谈谈了。

MARTIN:

I need some information about the actors. Who is that on the stage?

我需要有关演员的一些资料。现在在舞台上的是谁？

JOHN:

He's playing Macbeth.

他正在扮演麦克白。

MARTIN:

What's his name?

他叫什么名字？

JOHN:

His name is Paul Major. The man coming on stage now is playing Banquo.

他的名字叫保罗・梅杰。正向舞台走来的那个男的在扮演班柯。

MARTIN:

What's his name?

他叫什么名字？

JOHN:

His name is Winston Fishburn.

他叫温斯顿・费歇本。

MARTIN:

Could you show me around the theater?

你可以领我到剧院周围看看吗？

JOHN:

Sure. The auditorium is small. It has about 250 seats. The seats are new this year.

当然。观众席不大，有大约二百五十个座位。是今年新装的。

MARTIN:

The stage is large, isn't it?

舞台很大，是吗？

JOHN:

It's large for a summer theater. We can perform Shakespeare. We can perform large musicals too. This summer we are doing Kiss Me Kate.

对于夏日剧院而言，舞台是大的。我们可以表演莎士比亚的戏剧。我么还可以表演大型音乐剧。这个夏天我们要表演“吻我吧，凯蒂”。

MARTIN:

Have you directed a musical before?

你以前导演过音乐剧吗？

JOHN:

No. That's why we're doing Kiss Me Kate. It's based on Shakespeare you know. I've directed Shakespeare many times. We also had the costumes. It won't cost much.

没有。所以我们要上演“吻我吧，凯蒂”。那是根据莎士比亚的剧本改编的，你知道。我导演多很多莎士比亚的戏剧。我们还有那些服装。不会太破费的。

MARTIN:

Is cost a problem?

成本费用是个问题吧？

JOHN:

Always. Money is our biggest problem. We don't make any money in the summer.

历来如此。钱是我们最大的问题。我们这个夏天一点钱也赚不了。

MARTIN:

Do your actors receive a salary?

演员们都领工资吗？

JOHN:

Most of them. They are professional actors. A few are students. They don't receive a salary, but we pay their expenses.

大部分是。他们都是专业演员。有些是学生。学生不领工资，不过我们支付他们的开销。

MARTIN:

What kind of expenses?

什么样的开销？

JOHN:

Food and lodging. Excuse me Paul. This is Martin Learner. He's the reporter.

饮食和住宿。对不起，保罗。这位是马丁・雷纳。

MARTIN:

Hi. Don't let me interrupt. I'll talk to you later.

嗨。别让我打断你的排练。我待会儿找你谈。
