English USA Translation
Lesson 55, Part 2

L55P2J.MP3[488KB]

JOHN:

This is the backstage area.

这里是后台。

MARTIN:

It's very small, isn't it?

这后台很小，是吗？

JOHN:

Yes. We have a large stage. We can use a lot of actors. But the backstage area is too small. We can't move.

是的，我们的舞台很大。我们可以用很多演员。不过后台部分是太小了。我们无法搬迁。

MARTIN:

What do you do?

你是干什么的？

JOHN:

Come and see. This is the men's dressing room. We have 12 men and boys in the play.

过来看看。这是男士化妆室。演这出戏的有十二个男人和男孩。

JOHN:

How are you doing, everybody? This is Martin Learner.

各位都好吧？这位是马丁・雷纳。

MARTIN:

Thanks for letting me visit. How many minutes until the play begins?

谢谢你们让我来参观。还有几分钟开始演出？

JOHN:

Thirty minutes.

三十分钟。

MARTIN:

You're Macbeth, aren't you?

你是麦克白，是吗？

PAUL:

Right.

是的。

MARTIN:

You look different now.

你看上去是不同。

PAUL:

Good. Do I look like Macbeth?

好哇。我看上去像麦克白吗？

MARTIN:

Yes, you do.

是的，你像。

PAUL:

Last week I was a clown. In Twelfth Night.

上周我是个丑角，在《第十二个夜晚》那出戏里。

MARTIN:

How many plays are you in during the summer?

这个夏天你出演多少场戏呢？

PAUL:

We do three plays. We are in all of them. I have one big part. That's Macbeth, of course. I have small parts in the other plays.

我们演出三场戏。我们每场都有戏。我演的是主角。就是麦克白，不消说。在其他戏里，我出演一些小角色。

MARTIN:

This is the first night for Macbeth, isn't it? Are you afraid?

这是上演麦克白的第一晚，是吗？你害怕吗？

PAUL:

A little.

有一点。

JOHN:

Come meet Lady Macbeth, Martin.

来见见麦克白夫人，马丁。

MARTIN:

Thanks for talking with me, Paul. Goodbye.

谢谢你接受我的采访，保罗。再见。

JOHN:

Martin, this is Lady Macbeth.

马丁，这是麦克白夫人。

BETH:

Hello. We've met.

你好。我们见过面的。

JOHN:

Oh. Good. I'm going to leave you, Martin. I'll see you after the play. Beth, please introduce Martin to the others.

哦，好。我准备把你留在这，马丁。戏演完后我来看你。贝丝，请把马丁介绍给其他人。

MARTIN:

Thanks, John. See you later.

谢谢你，约翰。回头见。

BETH:

It's crowded, isn't it?

很拥挤，是吧？

MARTIN:

How are you doing?

你觉得怎么样？

BETH:

Very well, thank you.

很好，谢谢。

MARTIN:

Are you afraid?

你害怕吗？

BETH:

She's afraid. Wendy, this is Martin Learner. Wendy is playing Lady Macduff. It is her first time on the stage.

她害怕。文迪，这是马丁・雷纳。文迪扮演马克道夫太太。这是她第一次登台。

MARTIN:

And what's her name?

她叫什么名字？

BETH:

Her name is Moon.

她的名字是月亮。

MARTIN:

Pardon me.

请你再说一遍。

BETH:

Moon. You know, like moon, stars, sun. Moon.

月亮。你知道的，好像月亮，星星，太阳。她叫月亮。

MARTIN:

Wonderful.

好极了。

BETH:

Excuse me, Martin. I must finish.

很抱歉，马丁。我必须结束了。

MARTIN:

Thanks. I'm going to talk with the other women.

谢谢。我准备采访别的女士。
