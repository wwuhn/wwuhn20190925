English USA Translation
Lesson 49, Part 1

L49P1J.MP3[531KB]

EILEEN:

Martin, turn the radio down please.

马丁，请把收音机的音量调低一点。

EILEEN:

Are you going to have a busy day, Martin?

你今天会很忙吗，马丁？

MARTIN:

Not too busy. I'm going to finish a story.

不会太忙。我打算写完这篇报道。

EILEEN:

Sue, please call Alan. His breakfast is getting cold.

苏，你去叫阿兰。他的早餐要凉了。

MARTIN:

What are you doing today?

你今天要干什么？

SUE:

Alan! Hurry up! You're late.

阿兰！快一点！你迟到了。

EILEEN:

I'm going to be very busy today.

我今天会很忙的。

MARTIN:

Do you want me to shop today?

你想叫我去买东西吗？

EILEEN:

Can you? I can't come home until seven o'clock. Can you shop?

你能去吗？我要到七点才能回到家里。你能去购物吗？

MARTIN:

Sure.

当然了。

ALAN:

Good morning.

早上好。

EILEEN:

Good morning.

早上好。

MARTIN:

Morning, Alan.

早上好，阿兰。

ALAN:

I didn't sleep last night.

我昨晚没睡觉。

EILEEN:

Are you sick?

你生病了吗？

ALAN:

I don't think so. May I have some tea?

我看没有。我可以喝点茶吗？

EILEEN:

Eat something too.

再吃点什么吧。

MARTIN:

I'm going shopping today. What do you need?

我今天要去买东西。你们需要什么？

SUE:

Me? I need things for school. I need some notebooks. And I want a new pen. I don't like my pen.

我？我要上学用的东西。我需要笔记本。我还要一枝新钢笔。我不喜欢我（现在）的钢笔。

MARTIN:

I'm going to buy food. What food do you want?

我要买些食品。你想要什么食品？

ALAN:

I don't want any food.

我不想要任何食品。

MARTIN:

You are sick.

你生病了。

SUE:

He listened to his stereo all night.

他昨晚整夜都在听立体音响。

ALAN:

I did not.

我没有。

SUE:

I heard it. All night.

我听见了。整整一夜。

EILEEN:

Now, now. Let's help daddy. What do we need from the store?

好了，好了。我们帮帮父亲吧。我们需要从商店买些什么呢？

MARTIN:

What do you want, Eileen.

你想要什么，爱莲？

EILEEN:

Please buy some nice tomatoes.

请买一些好西红柿回来。

MARTIN:

OK. Anything else?

好的。还有别的吗？

EILEEN:

Buy some onions.

买一些洋葱。

MARTIN:

We need some milk.

我们需要一些牛奶。

EILEEN:

And bread.

还有面包。

SUE:

I want some new cereal. I don't like this cereal.

我想要一些新的谷类食品。我不喜欢这些谷类食品。

EILEEN:

You wanted it.

原本就是你要的。

SUE:

I don't like it.

我不喜欢这种。

EILEEN:

What do you want?

你想要什么？

SUE:

I don't know. Daddy, you buy some cereal.

我不知道。爸爸，你买一些谷类食品吧。

MARTIN:

Do we need any eggs?

我们需要鸡蛋吗？

ALAN:

Ugh. Don't talk about eggs.

喔。不要说鸡蛋。

SUE:

Yes, he's sick.

没错，他病了。

EILEEN:

I want some green vegetables too.

我也想要写绿色的蔬菜。

MARTIN:

Do we need any meat?

我们需要肉吗？

EILEEN:

No, we have some. Buy some fruit.

不需要，我们有了。买些水果吧。

MARTIN:

Sue, what kind of fruit do you want?

苏，你想要哪一种水果？

SUE:

Bananas.

香蕉。

MARTIN:

Alan, what kind of fruit do you want?

阿兰，你想要哪一种水果？

ALAN:

Oranges. Do we have any oranges now?

橙子。我们现在还有橙子吗？

EILEEN:

Yes. Do you want one?

有。来一个吗？

ALAN:

Please.

好的。

EILEEN:

Alan, you look terrible.

阿兰，你脸色和难看。

MARTIN:

You look sick.

你看上去像病了。

EILEEN:

Let me feel your head. You are sick. You're too hot. Do you want to go to the doctor?

让我摸摸你的头。你病了。你身上发烫。你想去看医生吗？

ALAN:

Not today. Can I stay home?

今天不去。我可以呆在家里吗？

EILEEN:

Of course.

当然可以。

SUE:

Can I stay home?

我可以呆在家里吗？

EILEEN:

Of course not.

当然不可以。
