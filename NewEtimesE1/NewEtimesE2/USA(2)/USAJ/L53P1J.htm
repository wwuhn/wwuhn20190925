English USA Translation
Lesson 53, Part 1

L53P1J.MP3[333KB]

MARTIN:

Good morning.

早上好。

BARBER:

Hello.

你好。

MARTIN:

Can I get a haircut?

我可以剪个发吗？

BARBER:

Can you wait for five minutes?

你可以等五分钟吗？

MARTIN:

Sure. I'm not in a hurry.

可以。我不急。

BARBER:

I'm finishing Bill now.

我就要帮比尔剪完头发啦。

BILL:

I'm Bill.

我是比尔。

MARTIN:

I'm Martin.

我是马丁。

BARBER:

Bill doesn't have much hair anyhow.

好歹比尔头发不算多。

BILL:

Do I get a discount?

我有折头吗？

BARBER:

A discount?! It's more work to cut your hair.

折头？跟你剪发更费事。

BILL:

Why?

为什么？

BARBER:

Because I have to look for it.

因为要帮你找头发来剪。

BILL:

Are you new in town, Martin?

你是新到镇里来的吗，马丁？

MARTIN:

I'm passing through.

我只是路过而已。

BARBER:

OK, Bill. That's it. How does it look?

好，比尔。剪好了。感觉怎样？

BILL:

Wow! I'm handsome.

 哇！我很英俊啊。

BARBER:

You still have to pay. Sit right here, Martin.

 你仍要付钱。坐这儿，马丁。

BILL:

Here you are.

 给你钱。

BARBER:

Thanks, Bill. See you soon.

谢谢，比尔。再见。

BILL:

Right. Are you staying in Smithville long, Martin?

再见。你会呆在斯密维尔很久吗，马丁？

MARTIN:

No, only one day.

不，只呆一天。

BILL:

Well, have a nice day.

好，祝你有愉快的一天。

MARTIN:

Thanks.

谢谢。

BILL:

So long.

再见。

MARTIN:

Bye.

再见。

BARBER:

My name's Joe.

我叫祖。

MARTIN:

I'm happy to meet you Joe. I'm happy to find a barber too. Can you trim my hair a little?

很高兴认识你，祖。我也很高兴找到一位理发师。你可以把我的头发修剪一下吗？

BARBER:

Sure. I can do anything you want.

当然可以。你想怎样都行。

BARBER:

What brings you to Smithville?

你为什么来斯密维尔？

MARTIN:

I'm a reporter. I'm on my way to Long Lake.

我是一个记者。我在去长湖的途中。

BARBER:

To the summer theater?

是去夏季剧场吗？

MARTIN:

Yes. I'm writing a story about the summer theater.

　

 是的。我正写一篇关于夏季剧场的文章。

BARBER:

It's quite famous.

那里很著名。

BOB:

Good morning, Joe.

早上好，祖。

BARBER:

Good morning, Bob. How are you?

早上好， 鲍勃。你怎么样？

BOB:

Fine thanks. How are you?

很好，谢谢。你呢？

BARBER:

Not bad. This is Martin.

也不错。这是马丁。

BOB:

Hello.

你好。

MARTIN:

Hi.

嗨。
