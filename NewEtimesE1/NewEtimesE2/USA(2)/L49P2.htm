English USA Lesson 49, Part 2

1.
　

续Part 1。学习表达想要什么东西或需要什么东西。马丁和家人在自己家里吃早餐。

L49P2_1.MP3[610KB]

This is English USA, on the Voice of America. Now, Lesson 49, Part 2.

MARTIN:

Sue, are you ready for school?

SUE:

Yes.

EILEEN:

Please finish your breakfast. We need to go.

SUE:

Are you taking me today, mama?

EILEEN:

Yes. We need to go in five minutes.

SUE:

OK. Do I have to eat this?

EILEEN:

Yes, please. It's good for you.

MARTIN:

Alan, does your head ache?

ALAN:

No. I'm tired.

MARTIN:

Do you need anything from the store?

ALAN:

No. Yes. Something to drink.

MARTIN:

What do you want?

ALAN:

Lemon soda.

MARTIN:

Right. Eileen, anything else?

EILEEN:

Did you write vegetables?

MARTIN:

Yes, I did. What kind of vegetables do you want?

EILEEN:

Green vegetables. Green beans. Peas. Broccoli. Any green vegetable.

MARTIN:

Do you want something for salad?

EILEEN:

Did you write tomatoes?

MARTIN:

Yes.

EILEEN:

Do you want lettuce?

MARTIN:

It's OK.

EILEEN:

Look at the store. Find something interesting.

SUE:

Do I have to eat salad?

EILEEN:

Don't talk about salad now. Finish your breakfast.

MARTIN:

Do we need coffee?

EILEEN:

Oh, yes! And we need sugar.

MARTIN:

Do we need tea?

EILEEN:

Not today. We have some.

MARTIN:

Do we need flour?

EILEEN:

I don't know. Please look.

MARTIN:

Yes, we need flour. Do you want brown or white flour?

EILEEN:

Both.

SUE:

I need some cookies. The cookies are finished.

EILEEN:

Finished?!

SUE:

Alan ate them.

ALAN:

No, I didn't.

EILEEN:

How many did you eat yesterday?

SUE:

A few.

EILEEN:

How many?

SUE:

Some.

EILEEN:

How many?

SUE:

Five. Alan ate a lot.

EILEEN:

Alan, how many did you eat?

ALAN:

I don't know. A lot.

MARTIN:

That's too many cookies. No more cookies this week. What
else do we need?

ALAN:

May I have some soup?

EILEEN:

Of course. We have some. What kind do you want?

ALAN:

Tomato.

MARTIN:

OK. Tomato soup. Anything else? What do you want for dinner tonight?

SUE:

Can we have pizza?

EILEEN:

That's a good idea.

MARTIN:

Do we have everything we need for pizza?

EILEEN:

Sure.

2.
　

学习“want”,“need”的句法。“want”表示“想要”，“need”表示“需要”、“有必要得到”。请注意期间的区别。

L49P2_2.MP3[40KB]

MARTIN:

Do you need anything from the store?

ALAN:

Yes.

MARTIN:

What do you want?

ALAN:

Lemon soda.

MARTIN:

What do you want for dinner tonight?

SUE:

Can we have pizza?

MARTIN:

Do we have everything we need for pizza?

3.
　

马丁问你想要什么东西，你根据自己的实际回答问题。然后是前一段对话的继续。

L49P2_3.MP3[254KB]

MARTIN:

What do you want to eat for breakfast?

YOU:

[I want to eat ----- for my breakfast.]

MARTIN:

What do you need to buy?

YOU:

[I need to buy -----.]

MARTIN:

What do you want to wear to work?

YOU:

[I want to wear ---- for work.]

MARTIN:

What do you need to buy?

YOU:

[I need to buy -----.]

EILEEN:

Sue. Let's go. I'm going to be late. Alan, please go to bed. Goodbye, Martin.

MARTIN:

Goodbye. See you tonight.
