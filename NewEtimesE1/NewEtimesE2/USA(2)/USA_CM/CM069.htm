English USA Lesson 69  学习表达有能力或有可能作某事的句型。马丁在表演现场跟导演和演员们讨论了有关美国土著居民的有文字记录的历史。

   Conversation Model

Female:

Could you go to the West or Southwest?

Martin:

Yes, I could.

Female:

When could you go?

Martin:

I could go any time. The sooner the better.

Female:

I'm checking the calendar. Would you go to South Dakota, or Montana, or Wyoming?

Martin:

Yes, I could.

Female:

Could I call you back in a few minutes?
