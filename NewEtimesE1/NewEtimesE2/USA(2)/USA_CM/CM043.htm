English USA Lesson 43：描述物体。

Conversation Model

　

Kelly:

I'm going to tell you about one car. It was black. And it was terrible.

　

Martin:

What do you mean terrible?

　

Kelly:

It was rusted. It was damaged. The paint was chipped. One window was broken. The tires were not good.
