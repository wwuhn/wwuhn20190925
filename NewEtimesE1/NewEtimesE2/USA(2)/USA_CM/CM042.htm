English USA Lesson 42：描述建筑物的位置、外观。

Conversation Model

　

Receptionist:

Do you see that tall glass building?

　

Martin:

The round building?

　

Receptionist:

Yes, that round building.

　

Martin:

The blue glass building?

　

Receptionist:

No. That's the Carter Building. Roosevelt Towers is near there.

　

Martin:

I can't see it.

　

Receptionist:

No, you can't see it from here. It's behind the Carter Building.
