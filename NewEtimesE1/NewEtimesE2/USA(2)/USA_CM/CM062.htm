English USA Lesson 62  学习理解指令和规定以及做事时求得允许的方法。马丁和两个朋友到弗吉尼亚州拜访美国第一位总统乔治•华盛顿的住所。

  Conversation Model

MAN:

May we look at the gardens?

MARTIN:

Yes, we may.

WOMAN:

May we take pictures?

GUIDE:

You may not take pictures in some places.

WOMAN:

May we see upstairs?

GUIDE:

Oh, yes, in a few minutes.

MALE:

May we smoke?

GUIDE:

No. Please don't smoke. It's dangerous.
