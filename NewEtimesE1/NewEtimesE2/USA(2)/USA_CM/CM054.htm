English USA Lesson 54  学习在非正式场合下与人会面的自我介绍。马丁到酒吧去，在那跟彼此刚见面相识的人一起聊天。

   Conversation Model

Beth:

Goodbye for now.

Gloria:

See you.

Martin:

Good night.

Rick:

Good night, Gloria. Bye, Beth.

Rick:

Hey, Tony. How is the family? Are they back from
their trip?

Tony:

Yes. They came yesterday.

Rick:

Jackson, how are you feeling?

Jackson:

Not bad.

Rick:

How are you doing?

Sally:

OK. I love this weather.
