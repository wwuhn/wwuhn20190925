English USA Lesson 57  学习表述年龄和住址。马丁和附近的居民到公园里看美化当地公园的请愿书。他们对到公园里来的人读请愿书，请他们签名支持。

   Conversation Model

Martin:

Do you live in the neighborhood?

Roberta:

Yes. I live at 128 36th Street.

Martin:

When were you born?

Roberta:

I was born in 1922.

Martin:

How long have you lived in this neighborhood?

Roberta:

Oh, about 50 years.

Martin:

How old are you?

Olive:

It's none of your business.

Roberta:

She's 75 years old.

Martin:

Where do you live?

Olive:

I live at 1783 Parkway.
