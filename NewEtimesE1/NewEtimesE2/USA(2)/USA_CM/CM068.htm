   Conversation Model

Martin:

May I ask some questions, Lieutenant Lucas?

Lucas:

Of course.

Martin:

May I ask some questions?

Lucas:

Sure. Take your time.

Martin:

The man who was playing that game was very young.

Lucas:

Most of the men are very young.

Martin:

What does he do?

Lucas:

He's the one who sends messages.
