English USA Lesson 63  复习家庭成员和亲属的关系称谓。马丁采访了一家马戏团，听取了表演马戏的那一家人的故事。

   Conversation Model

Martin:

Hello. Are all of these your children?

Todd:

No, no. Some are nieces and nephews. These are my children. All the others are our nieces and nephews. The children are cousins.

Todd:

This old man is my father-in-law. And this beautiful lady is my mother-in-law.

Martin:

Whose niece are you?

Niece:

I'm uncle Jerry's niece. And this is my brother. He's uncle Jerry's nephew.

Nephew:

I'm aunt Amy's nephew too.
