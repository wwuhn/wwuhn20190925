English USA Lesson 60, Part 1

1.

　

学习与人的所有物或持有物有关的表达法。马丁准备写一篇职业女性的报道，与一位妇女约了访谈时间，但是约定访谈的那位妇女所在的公司前一天晚上被小偷入室盗窃，办公室里物品凌乱。

L60P1_1.MP3[604KB]

This is English USA, on the Voice of America. Now, Lesson 60, Part 1.

POLICEMAN:

Quiet, please.

POLICEMAN:

May I have your attention? Quiet, please.

MAN 1:

Who are you?

JENNIFER:

Are you the police? We've had burglars!

POLICEMAN:

Yes, we're the police. I'm Detective Hansen. Those two men and that woman are going to look around the office. I want to ask you some questions.

JENNIFER:

We have never been burglarized before.

POLICEMAN:

Unfortunately, we've seen many burglaries.

MAN 1:

In offices?

POLICEMAN:

In offices, homes, stores. Everywhere. I've been investigating burglaries for ten years.

JENNIFER:

We might have been killed.

POLICEMAN:

I don't think so. Burglars don't want to hurt or kill anyone. They come at night or on the weekend.

MAN 1:

Sometimes we work at night.

JENNIFER:

Or on Saturday.

POLICEMAN:

I know. It can be a problem. Then people can get hurt.

WOMAN:

And look at the mess.

MAN 2:

They threw everything all together.

WOMAN:

They put all the papers and boxes and books here. This is my coffee cup.

JENNIFER:

And this is my basket.

MAN 1:

It's a mess.

POLICEMAN:

Whose cup is this?

MAN 1:

It's mine.

POLICEMAN:

Whose papers are these?

JENNIFER:

Let me see. They're mine.

WOMAN:

Those books are mine.

MAN 1:

This is my pen. It has my name on it.

MARTIN:

Excuse me. I found this briefcase in the stairway.

POLICEMAN:

Who are you?

MARTIN:

I'm Martin Learner.

POLICEMAN:

Do you work in this office?

MARTIN:

No. I'm looking for Miss Jennifer Donnelly. What's going on?

JENNIFER:

We've had a burglar.

WOMAN:

Last night. They took my computer.

JENNIFER:

I'm Jennifer Donnelly. We had an appointment, didn't we?

MARTIN:

Yes, at nine o'clock. I'm sorry I'm late.

JENNIFER:

Don't worry. Look at this mess.

MARTIN:

I can come back later.

JENNIFER:

That's all right. We can talk in my office.

MARTIN:

OK.

POLICEMAN:

OK, everyone. Go to work. Go to your offices. I want to talk to you one by one.

JENNIFER:

Come in Mr. Learner. This is my office.

2.
　

自己持有的物品用人称代词“I”的所有格“my”+名词来表达，例如“my pen”。

L60P1_2.MP3[23KB]

WOMAN:

This is my coffee cup.

JENNIFER:

And this is my basket.

MAN 1:

This is my pen.

3.
　

下面请你将马丁说的话用“This is my______.”首先是马丁和一位妇女为你举例。练习之后是前段对话的继续。

L60P1_3.MP3[279KB]

MARTIN:

It's a coffee cup.

WOMAN:

This is my coffee cup.

MARTIN:

It's a pen.

YOU:

[This is my pen.]

MARTIN:

It's a basket.

YOU:

[This is my basket.]

MARTIN:

It's a book.

YOU:

[This is my book.]

POLICEMAN:

Excuse me. May I come in? I want to ask some questions.

JENNIFER:

Of course.

POLICEMAN:

Whose tape recorder is this?

MARTIN:

That's my tape recorder. I'm sorry. I left it on the desk outside.

POLICEMAN:

Is this your briefcase?

MARTIN:

No, it's not mine.

JENNIFER:

Maybe it's Rollin's briefcase. It looks like his. Would you like some coffee, Detective Hansen.

POLICEMAN:

Thanks, but the burglar took the coffee machine.

JENNIFER:

The coffee machine! Why would a burglar take a coffee machine?

POLICEMAN:

He can sell it.

MARTIN:

Or he likes coffee.

POLICEMAN:

I'll come back in a few minutes.
