Song       Long, Long Ago              cloze     

    

    Tell me the tales
    That to me were so dear,
    Long, long ago,
    Long, long ago;
    Sing me the songs
    I delighted to hear,
    Long, long ago,
    Long ago.
    Now you are come,
    All my grief is removed,
    Let me forget
    That so long you have roved,
    Let me believe
    That you love as you loved,
    Long, long ago,
    Long ago.
    Do you remember
    The path where we met,
    Long, long ago,
    Long, long ago?
    That's when you told me
    You would not forget,
    Long, long ago,
    Long ago.
    Then, to all others
    My smile you preferred,
    Love, when you spoke,
    Gave a charm to each word,
    Still my heart treasures
    The praises I heard,
    Long, long ago,
    Long ago.
    Though by your kindness
    My fond hopes were raised,
    Long, long ago,
    Long, long ago;
    You by more eloquent
    Lips have been praised,
    Long, long ago,
    Long ago.
    But by long absence
    Your truth has been tried,
    Still to your accents
    I listen with pride,
    Blessed as I was
    When I sat by your side,
    Long, long ago,
    Long ago.
