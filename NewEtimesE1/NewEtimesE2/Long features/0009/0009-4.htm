著名美国诗人罗伯特・福斯特（1）

Exploration Tasks

  • In groups of 4 to 6, ask 10 to 12 special comprehension questions beginning with why, who, how, etc.
  • Use the above questions as a guideline to make a summery of the text.
  • Do you happen to know any other American poets and their poems? Have  you ever read their poems? Which one do you like best? And why? Can you recite the poems? Group discussion.
