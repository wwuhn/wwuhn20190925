Exploration Tasks

  • In groups of 4 to 6, ask 10 to 12 special comprehension questions beginning with why, who, how, etc.
  • Use the above questions as a guideline to make a summery of the text.
  • Do you happen to read about any Chinese weddings? If yes, compare them with what you studied in this text. How do they differ and what do they share in common? Group discussion.
