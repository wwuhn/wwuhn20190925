Exploration Tasks

  • In groups of 4 to 6, ask 10 to 12 special comprehension questions beginning with why, who, how, etc.
  • Use the above questions as a guideline to make a summery of the text.
  • What good do summer camps do to Children? Group discussion.
  • Are summer camps common in China? Why or why not? How much do you think you know about them in China? Group discussion.

　

Annotation
