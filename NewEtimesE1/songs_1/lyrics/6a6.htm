奔放的旋律     Unchained Melody     cloze

    

    Oh, my love, my darling, I've hungered for your touch a long, lonely time. And time goes by so slowly, and time can do so much. Are you still mine? I need your love, I need your love. God speed your love to me. Lonely rivers sigh, "Wait for me, wait for me." I'll be coming home, wait for me.
