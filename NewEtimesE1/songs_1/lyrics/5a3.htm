黄河镇     Yellow River     cloze

    

    So long, boy. You can take my place. Got my papers; I got my pay. So pack my bags and I'll be on my way to Yellow River.
    Put my gun down; the war is won. Fill my glass. Ah, the time has come. I'm going back to the place that I love-Yellow River.
    Yellow River, Yellow River is in my mind and in my eyes. Yellow River, Yellow River is in my blood-it's the place I love. Got no time for explanations. Got no time to lose. Tomorrow night you'll find me sleeping underneath the moon at Yellow River.
    Cannon fire lingers in my mind. I'm so glad I'm still alive. And I've been gone for such a long time from Yellow River.
    I remember how the nights were cool. I can still see the water move. And I remember the girl that I knew from Yellow River.
