English USA Lesson 25：过去时疑问句。

Conversation Model

Martin:

When did you work in New York?

Doctor:

Five years ago. But I visited New York too.

Martin:

When did you visit New York?

Doctor:

Ten years ago. Three years ago. And last year.

Martin:

When do you visit family in Georgia?

Doctor:

I visit my family during my vacation.

Martin:

When did you visit your family?

Doctor:

I visited my family last summer.
