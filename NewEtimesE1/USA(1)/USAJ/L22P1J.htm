EILEEN:

Hello.

　

　

嗨。

　

MARTIN:

Hello, Eileen.

　

　

嗨，艾琳。

　

EILEEN:

Martin! Hello.

　

　

马丁！你好。

　

MARTIN:

How are you?

　

　

你好吗？

　

EILEEN:

Fine. How are you?

　

　

很好。你好吗？

　

MARTIN:

OK. What are you doing?

　

　

还可以。你在干什么？

　

EILEEN:

We're eating.

　

　

我们在吃饭。

　

MARTIN:

Can you talk now?

　

　

你能再说点什么吗？

　

EILEEN:

Oh, sure. Where are you?

　

　

当然。你在哪里？

　

MARTIN:

I'm in Boston. I'm coming home on Thursday.

　

　

我在波士顿。我周二回家去。

　

EILEEN:

Wonderful. What are you doing?

　

　

太好了。你在干什么？

　

MARTIN:

I'm watching television.

　

　

我再看电视。

　

EILEEN:

Did you go to the baseball game?

　

　

你去看棒球赛了吗？

　

MARTIN:

I went to the baseball game on Sunday afternoon.

　

　

我周日下午去了。

　

EILEEN:

Did you talk to the players?

　

　

你和选手谈话了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes. I talked to some players. It was very interesting. I recorded some players.

　

　

是的。我采访了一些选手。很有意思。我还给一些选手录了音。

　

EILEEN:

Are you writing your story?

　

　

你再写你的报道吗？

　

MARTIN:

I wrote it on Monday. May I speak to the children?

　

　

我周一写了。我可以和孩子们说话吗？

　

EILEEN:

Of course. Just a minute. Alan, it's your father.

　

　

当然。等一下。艾伦，是你爸爸。

　

ALAN:

Hi, dad.

　

　

你好，爸爸。

　

MARTIN:

Hi, Alan. What are you doing?

　

　

你好，艾伦。你在干什么？

　

ALAN:

Nothing.

　

　

没干什么。

　

MARTIN:

Nothing? What did you do on Sunday? Did you go to the movies?

　

　

没干什么？你们周日干了什么？你们去看电影了吗？

　

ALAN:

No, we didn't go. I didn't have any money.

　

　

没有，我们没去。我没有钱。

　

MARTIN:

What did you do?

　

　

那你们干什么了？

　

ALAN:

We watched television.

　

　

我们看电视了。

　

MARTIN:

What did you do on Monday?

　

　

你周一干什么了？

　

ALAN:

I worked.

　

　

我干活了。

　

MARTIN:

Did you go swimming?

　

　

你们去游泳了吗？

　

ALAN:

Yes. We went swimming in the evening.

　

　

是的。我们晚上去游泳了。

　

MARTIN:

Who went swimming?

　

　

谁去游泳了？

　

ALAN:

I went with my friends.

　

　

我和我的朋友去游泳了。

　

MARTIN:

Did Sue go swimming?

　

　

秀去了吗？

　

ALAN:

No, she didn't.

　

　

她没去。

　

MARTIN:

What did you do today?

　

　

你今天干什么了？

　

ALAN:

I worked in the morning.

　

　

早上我干活了。

　

MARTIN:

Do you like your work?

　

　

你喜欢这份工吗？

　

ALAN:

It's OK.

　

　

还可以吧。

　

MARTIN:

What are you doing now?

　

　

现在你在干什么？

　

ALAN:

We're eating.

　

　

我们再吃东西。

　

MARTIN:

May I talk to Sue?

　

　

我可以和秀说说吗？

　

ALAN:

Sure. Sue! Dad wants to talk to you. Bye, dad.

　

　

秀。秀！爸爸想和你说话。再见，爸爸。

　

MARTIN:

Goodbye. I'm coming home on Thursday.

　

　

再见。我周四就回家。

　

SUE:

Hello, daddy.

　

　

嗨，爸爸。

　

MARTIN:

Hi, Sue. How are you?

　

　

嗨，秀。你好吗？

　

SUE:

Fine.

　

　

很好。

　

MARTIN:

What are you doing?

　

　

你在干什么？

　

SUE:

We're eating.

　

　

我们在吃东西。

　

MARTIN:

What are you eating?

　

　

你在吃什么？

　

SUE:

I don't know.

　

　

我不知道。

　

MARTIN:

You don't know!

　

　

你不知道！

　

SUE:

Let's see. Green beans.

　

　

让我看看。绿豆。

　

MARTIN:

That's all?!

　

　

只有绿豆？！

　

SUE:

No. We have meat and potatoes. We had pizza last night.

　

　

不是。我们吃肉和土豆。我们做完吃了比萨饼。

　

MARTIN:

And you were very happy.

　

　

那你就非常高兴了。

　

SUE:

Yes. I love pizza.

　

　

是的。我喜欢吃比萨饼。

　

MARTIN:

Sue, may I speak to your mother?

　

　

秀，我可以和你妈妈说话吗？

　

SUE:

OK. Bye, daddy.

　

　

好的。再见，爸爸。

　

MARTIN:

Goodbye.

　

　

再见。

Hi.

　

　

嗨。

你周一干了什么？

　

EILEEN:

Did you buy anything?

　

　

你买什么东西了吗？

　

EILEEN:

Yes. Sue went with me. We bought some clothes.

　

　

是的。秀和我一起去的。我们买了一些衣服

　

MARTIN:

I went to the movies last night.

　

　

昨晚我去看电影了。

Did you go to the museum?

　

　

你去博物馆了？

No, I didn't. Are you going to meet me at the airport on Thursday?

　

　

没有。周四你们 会来机场接我吗？

Yes.

　

　

会的。

好的。再见。
