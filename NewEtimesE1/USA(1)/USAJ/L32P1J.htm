English USA Translation

EILEEN:

Did you finish your work?

　

　

你做完工作了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I did. Where are the children?

　

　

做完了。孩子们呢？

　

EILEEN:

Sue is reading. Alan is dressing.

　

　

秀在看书，艾伦在穿衣服。

　

MARTIN:

Dressing?

　

　

穿衣服？

　

EILEEN:

For the party. He's going to a party tonight.

　

　

去参加晚会。他今天晚上要去参加晚会。

　

MARTIN:

Oh, yes. That's right. When is the party?

　

　

啊，对。晚会什么时候开始？

　

EILEEN:

The party begins at ten o'clock.

　

　

10点钟开始。

　

MARTIN:

Is he dressing now? It's only seven o'clock.

　

　

他现在正在穿衣服吗？现在只有7点钟。

　

EILEEN:

I know. But he's dressing. The party is important. Martin, are you going to take Sue to the movies tomorrow?

　

　

我知道。但是他正在穿衣服。这个晚会很重要。马丁，你明天要带秀去看电影吗？

　

MARTIN:

I can. Does she want to go tomorrow?

　

　

可以。她明天想去吗？

　

EILEEN:

Yes, she does. When is your meeting tomorrow?

　

　

是的。你明天什么时候开会？

　

MARTIN:

In the morning.

　

　

上午。

　

EILEEN:

When is the meeting?

　

　

什么时候的会？

　

MARTIN:

At eleven o'clock.

　

　

11点钟。

　

EILEEN:

Can you go to the movies in the afternoon?

　

　

你下午可以去看电影吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I can. When?

　

　

可以。什么时候？

　

EILEEN:

At four o'clock.

　

　

4点钟。

　

MARTIN:

OK.

　

　

好的。

　

EILEEN:

Sue wants to go to Jennifer's house tomorrow. She can go in the morning.

　

　

秀明天想去詹尼弗家。她明天可以去。

　

MARTIN:

When is dinner?

　

　

晚饭什么时候？

　

EILEEN:

You didn't eat lunch. Do you want dinner?

　

　

你没有吃午饭。你想吃晚饭吗？

　

MARTIN:

Very much!

　

　

很想！

　

EILEEN:

Dinner is at seven-thirty.

　

　

晚饭7：30。

　

SUE:

Hi. May I come in?

　

　

嗨，我可以进来吗？

　

MARTIN:

Of course.

　

　

当然了。

　

SUE:

Did you finish your work?

　

　

你做完工作了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I did.

　

　

是的。

　

SUE:

Can we go to the movies tomorrow?

　

　

我们明天可以去看电影吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, we can.

　

　

可以。

　

SUE:

When?

　

　

什么时候？

　

MARTIN:

In the afternoon.

　

　

下午。

　

SUE:

Can we go at six o'clock? I want to go to Jennifer's house.

　

　

我们6：00去可以吗？我想去詹尼弗家。

　

MARTIN:

That's very late. You have school on Monday. Let's go at four o'clock.

　

　

那会很晚的。你周一要上学。我们4点去吧。

　

SUE:

When can I go to Jennifer's house?

　

　

那我什么时候可以去詹尼弗家？

　

EILEEN:

You can go to Jennifer's house in the morning.

　

　

你可以上午去。

　

SUE:

May I phone Jennifer now?

　

　

我现在可以给詹尼弗打电话吗？

　

EILEEN:

No. It's time for dinner.

　

　

不行。现在要吃晚饭了。

　

SUE:

When may I phone her?

　

　

那我什么时候给她打电话？

　

EILEEN:

After dinner.

　

　

晚饭后。

　

SUE:

When is dinner?

　

　

什么时候吃饭？

　

EILEEN:

At seven-thirty.

　

　

7：30。

　

MARTIN:

In a few minutes.

　

　

几分钟后。

　

SUE:

OK. I'm going to read.

　

　

好吧。我要看书了。

　

EILEEN:

I'm going to finish dinner. Come help me.

　

　

我要做好饭，来帮我。

　

MARTIN:

OK.

　

　

好的。

　

　

　

　

EILEEN:

I'm going to call the children.

　

　

我去叫孩子们。

　

MARTIN:

I'm going to get some water.

　

　

我去拿点水。

　

EILEEN:

OK. Alan! Sue!

　

　

好的。艾伦！秀！
