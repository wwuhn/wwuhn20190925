English USA Translation
　

L16P1J.MP3[543KB]

　

MARTIN:

Thank you. Thank you very much. That was wonderful.

　

　

谢谢。谢谢。真是太棒了。

　

MUSICIAN:

You're welcome. Do you like the violin?

　

　

不客气。你喜欢小提琴吗？

　

MARTIN:

I love the violin.

　

　

非常喜欢。

　

MUSICIAN:

Would you like some coffee or tea?

　

　

你想喝点咖啡还是茶？

　

MARTIN:

No, thanks. I want to ask you some questions.

　

　

不用，谢谢。我想问你几个问题。

　

MUSICIAN:

OK.

　

　

可以。

　

MARTIN:

You're a young musician. How old are you?

　

　

你是一位年轻的音乐人。你多大了？

　

MUSICIAN:

I'm twenty-one years old.

　

　

二十一岁。

　

MARTIN:

Do you have a family?

　

　

你有家吗？

　

MUSICIAN:

I have a family in Finland. I don't have a family here.

　

　

我的家在芬兰，不在这里。

　

MARTIN:

Do you work? Do you have a job?

　

　

你有工作吗？

　

MUSICIAN:

I'm a musician. I don't work now. I'm a student.

　

　

我是音乐人。我现在不工作。我是一名学生。

　

MARTIN:

Do you study at the university?

　

　

你在读大学吗？

　

MUSICIAN:

Yes, I do.

　

　

对。

　

MARTIN:

Do you practice at the university?

　

　

你在大学练习吗？

　

MUSICIAN:

Yes. I practice at home too.

　

　

是的。我在家里也练习。

　

MARTIN:

Where do you live?

　

　

你住哪里？

　

MUSICIAN:

I live here. This is my home. Where do you live?

　

　

这里。我家就在这里。你呢？

　

MARTIN:

I live in Baltimore.

　

　

我住巴尔的摩。

　

MUSICIAN:

Do you live in an apartment?

　

　

你住在公寓里吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I do.

　

　

是的。

　

MUSICIAN:

This apartment is very small.

　

　

这座公寓很小。

　

MARTIN:

I live in a small apartment too. It's my home.

　

　

我也住在一座小公寓里。那是我的家。

　

MUSICIAN:

I live in a big house in Finland. But this is my home here.

　

　

我在芬兰住在一间大房子里。但这里现在是我的家。

　

MARTIN:

Did you play the violin in Finland?

　

　

你在芬兰拉小提琴吗？

　

MUSICIAN:

Oh, yes, I did. I played for many years.

　

　

是的。我已经拉了很多年了。

　

MARTIN:

Where did you play?

　

　

你在哪里拉小提琴？

　

MUSICIAN:

I played at school. And I played at home. My mother played the violin.

　

　

在学校，在家里。我母亲也拉小提琴。

　

MARTIN:

Did you play another musical instrument?

　

　

你以前还演奏过其它乐器吗？

　

MUSICIAN:

First, I played the piano. My father played the piano.

　

　

开始我弹钢琴。我父亲是弹钢琴的。

　

MARTIN:

Do you play the piano now?

　

　

你现在还弹吗？

　

MUSICIAN:

No, I don't. I don't study. I don't practice the piano now.

　

　

不弹了，现在我不学钢琴了，也不练习了。

　

MARTIN:

When did you play the piano?

　

　

你什么时候弹过钢琴？

　

MUSICIAN:

When I lived in Finland.

　

　

当我在芬兰的时候。

　

MARTIN:

How long did you play?

　

　

弹了多长时间？

　

MUSICIAN:

Ten years.

　

　

十年。

　

MUSICIAN:

Excuse me. Hello, Manny. Come in.

　

　

不好意思。嗨， 曼尼，进来。

　

MANNY:

Hi.

　

　

嗨。

　

MARTIN:

Hello, I'm Martin Learner. How are you?

　

　

嗨，我叫马丁・勒纳。你好吗？

　

MANNY:

Hi, I'm Manny.

　

　

嗨，我叫曼尼。

　

MUSICIAN:

Mr. Learner is a reporter. Manny is my friend.

　

　

勒纳先生是一位记者。曼尼是我的朋友。

　

MARTIN:

I'm happy to meet you.

　

　

见到你很高兴。

　

MANNY:

Thanks.

　

　

谢谢。

　

MARTIN:

Where is your home?

　

　

你家在哪里？

　

MANNY:

Here.

　

　

就在这里。

　

MARTIN:

You live here in this apartment?

　

　

你住在这里的公寓吗？

　

MANNY:

No, no. I don't live in this apartment. I live here in Bloomington. My home is in Bloomington.

　

　

不，我不住这座公寓。我住在这里，布龙明堂。我家在布龙明堂。

　

MUSICIAN:

My home is very small.

　

　

我的家很小。

　

MANNY:

My home is very small too.

　

　

我家也很小。
