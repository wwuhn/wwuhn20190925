WALEED:

Lee, your son is very handsome and your sister is very pretty.

　

　

李，你的儿子很英俊，你妹妹很漂亮。

　

LEE:

We're a handsome family.

　

　

我们全家都很漂亮。

　

MARTIN:

Some are handsome and some are not.

　

　

有些是，有些不是。

　

LEE:

Do you see that player over there?

　

　

你看到那边的那位球员了吗？

　

MARTIN:

The very big player?

　

　

那个大个的球员吗？

　

LEE:

Yes. He's the one with long legs and big arms.

　

　

 是的。他是唯一腿和胳膊长的。

　

WALEED:

This is a football game. There are many players with long legs and big arms.

　

　

这是足球赛。有很多腿和胳膊长的球员。

　

LEE:

I know. Do you see that short player? Look behind the short player. Now do you see the one with long legs and big arms?

　

　

我知道。你看到那个矮个子球员了吗？看他后面那个球员。现在你看到腿和胳膊长的哪个球员了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Maybe.

　

　

 也许吧。

　

LEE:

Who is he?

　

　

他是谁？

　

WALEED:

He's a new player.

　

　

他是一名新球员。

　

MARTIN:

I don't know his name. He came from Dallas.

　

　

我不知道他的名字。他来自达拉斯。

　

LEE:

Would you like something to eat?

　

　

你想吃点什么吗？

　

WALEED:

Something to drink.

　

　

我想喝点什么。

　

　

　

　

WALEED:

Martin, does your son play football?

　

　

马丁，你儿子踢足球吗？

　

MARTIN:

He's not on the team. I don't want him to play football. He's too small.

　

　

他不在足球队。我不想让他踢足球。他长得太矮了。

　

WALEED:

He isn't very small. He's tall.

　

　

他不矮，他长得挺高。

　

MARTIN:

But he's too thin.

　

　

但他太瘦了。

　

WALEED:

There's Lee over there. Who is that?

　

　

李在那边。那是谁？

　

MARTIN:

I can't see him. Is it his brother?

　

　

我看不见。那是他兄弟吗？

　

WALEED:

No, it isn't.

　

　

不是。

　

MARTIN:

What does he look like?

　

　

他长什么样？

　

WALEED:

He's not very tall.

　

　

不是很高。

　

MARTIN:

What color is his hair?

　

　

他的头发是什么颜色？

　

WALEED:

I can't see his hair. He has a cap. Come here. Can you see him now?

　

　

我看不到他的头发。他戴着帽子。到这里来，现在你可以看到他吗？

　

MARTIN:

Is he the one with a red cap?

　

　

是戴红帽子的那个吗?

　

WALEED:

Yes, that's the man.

　

　

是的，就是那个人。

　

MARTIN:

It looks like Lee's brother.

　

　

看上去像是李的兄弟。

　

WALEED:

His brother is sitting with Lee's son.

　

　

他的兄弟正和李的儿子坐在一起。

　

　

　

　

LEE:

OK. I have hot dogs.

　

　

好，我要热狗。

　

MARTIN:

What do you have to drink?

　

　

你要什么喝的？

　

LEE:

I have two beers and one coke.

　

　

我要两杯啤酒，一杯可乐。

　

WALEED:

May I have the coke?

　

　

我可以喝一杯可乐吗？

　

LEE:

Of course.

　

　

当然。

　

MARTIN:

Who was the man in the red cap?

　

　

戴红帽子的那个人是谁？

　

LEE:

That's my uncle. He loves football. He comes every game.

　

　

那是我叔叔。她喜欢足球。 每场比赛他都来。

　

WALEED:

He looks like your brother.

　

　

他很像你的兄弟。

　

MARTIN:

He doesn't look like you.

　

　

他不像你。

　

LEE:

That's right. He's the handsome uncle. Let's eat.

　

　

没错。他是一个英俊的叔叔。我们吃吧。

　

MARTIN:

Did you play football, Waleed?

　

　

你以前踢足球吗，瓦立德？

　

WALEED:

No. I played soccer. My family likes soccer.

　

　

没有，我踢英式足球。我的家人喜欢英式足球。 

　

LEE:

My family loves football.

　

　

我我们全家也喜欢英式足球。
