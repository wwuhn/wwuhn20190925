MOTHER:

Hello.

　

　

嗨。

　

MARTIN:

Hello, mother.

　

　

嗨，妈妈。

　

MOTHER:

Martin! This is a nice surprise.

　

　

马丁！真是太意外了。

　

MARTIN:

How are you?

　

　

你好吗？

　

MOTHER:

Fine. How are you?

　

　

很好。你呢？

　

MARTIN:

Fine thanks. How is father?

　

　

很好，谢谢。爸爸好吗？

　

MOTHER:

He's fine.

　

　

他很好。

　

MARTIN:

Is he there?

　

　

他在那边吗？

　

MOTHER:

Yes. How is Eileen?

　

　

在。艾琳好吗？

　

MARTIN:

She's fine.

　

　

她很好。

　

MOTHER:

How are the children?

　

　

孩子们好吗？

　

MARTIN:

They're fine.

　

　

他们都很好。

　

MOTHER:

May I talk to them?

　

　

我可以和他们说话吗？

　

MARTIN:

No. I'm not at home.

　

　

不，我不在家。

　

MOTHER:

Where are you?

　

　

你在哪里？

　

MARTIN:

I'm in Boston. I'm writing a story about baseball.

　

　

我在波士顿。我正在写一篇关于棒球的报道。

　

MOTHER:

That's very nice. Did you talk to the players?

　

　

真棒。你和选手们谈了吗？

　

MARTIN:

Yes, I did. What are you doing?

　

　

谈过了。你在做什么？

　

MOTHER:

We're painting the house.

　

　

我们在漆房子。

　

MARTIN:

Who is painting?

　

　

谁在漆房子？

　

MOTHER:

Your father is painting outside. I'm painting inside.

　

　

你爸爸在外面漆房子。我在里面漆。

　

MARTIN:

Can you do that?

　

　

你能做吗？

　

MOTHER:

Yes. It's a small house.

　

　

可以。房子很小。

　

MARTIN:

But you're not young.

　

　

但你不再年轻了。

　

MOTHER:

Your brother Phillip is coming on Saturday. He's going to help.

　

　

你哥哥菲利普周六来。他要来帮忙。

　

MARTIN:

That's good. Did you go to the park last week?

　

　

好。你们上周去公园了吗？

　

MOTHER:

No, we didn't. It's too expensive. We worked in the garden.

　

　

没有。太贵了。我们在花园里干活了。

　

MARTIN:

I phoned on Saturday. Where were you?

　

　

我周六打过电话。你们去哪里了？

　

MOTHER:

We went shopping.

　

　

我们买东西去了。

　

MARTIN:

What did you buy?

　

　

买了什么？

　

MOTHER:

We bought paint.

　

　

我们买了涂料。

　

MARTIN:

May I talk to father?

　

　

我能和爸爸说话吗？

　

MOTHER:

Yes. Here he is.

　

　

可以。他来了。

　

FATHER:

Hello, Martin.

　

　

嗨，马丁。

　

MARTIN:

Hello, how are you?

　

　

嗨。你好吗？

　

FATHER:

Fine. Are you coming to visit?

　

　

很好。你要来看我们吗？

　

MARTIN:

No. I'm working.

　

　

不行。我在工作。

　

FATHER:

Where are you?

　

　

你在哪里？

　

MARTIN:

I'm in Boston. I'm writing a story about baseball.

　

　

我在波士顿。我正在写一篇关于棒球的报道。

　

FATHER:

Boston? I like Chicago.

　

　

波士顿？我喜欢芝加哥。

　

MARTIN:

I know. Did you see the baseball game on Saturday?

　

　

我知道。你看了周六的棒球赛吗？

　

FATHER:

Yes. I watched the game on television.

　

　

是的。我在电视上看了比赛。

　

MARTIN:

Did you like the game?

　

　

你喜欢那场比赛吗？

　

FATHER:

No!!

　

　

不喜欢！！。

　

FATHER:

Martin, how are the children?

　

　

马丁，孩子们好吗？

　

MARTIN:

They're fine.

　

　

他们都很好。

　

FATHER:

How is Eileen?

　

　

艾琳好吗？

　

MARTIN:

She's fine. How is Phillip?

　

　

她也很好。菲利普好吗？

　

FATHER:

He's OK. He's coming on Saturday. We're going to paint.

　

　

很好。他周六来。我们要油漆房子。

　

MARTIN:

Are you painting?

　

　

你正在漆房子吗？

　

FATHER:

Yes, I am.

　

　

对。

　

MARTIN:

Can you do that?

　

　

你能做吗？

　

FATHER:

Yes, I can. I'm fine. Phillip's going to help. Did you go to California?

　

　

我行。我很好。菲利普要来帮忙。你去过加利福尼亚吗？

　

MARTIN:

No, I didn't.

　

　

没有。

　

FATHER:

Did you go to Nebraska?

　

　

你去过内布拉斯加马？

　

MARTIN:

No, I didn't. I went to Iowa.

　

　

没有。我去了爱荷华。

　

FATHER:

You went to Iowa, but didn't come to Chicago?

　

　

你去了爱荷华，但没有来芝加哥？

　

MARTIN:

I came to the airport. I was going to Iowa.

　

　

那时我去机场，要去爱荷华。

　

FATHER:

You didn't phone.

　

　

但你没打电话。

　

MARTIN:

No, I didn't.

　

　

没有。

　

FATHER:

Excuse me. Your mother wants to talk. Here she is. Goodbye.

　

　

失陪了。你妈妈 想和你说。她来了。再见。

　

MARTIN:

Goodbye, father.

　

　

再见，爸爸。

　

MOTHER:

Martin, tell Eileen to phone.

　

　

马丁，让艾琳打电话。

　

MARTIN:

You can phone her.

　

　

你可以给她打。

　

MOTHER:

Is she working?

　

　

她在上班吗？

　

MARTIN:

Of course. She doesn't work in the evening. You can phone in the evening.

　

　

当然。她晚上不上班。你可以晚上打。

　

MOTHER:

OK. Can I phone this evening?

　

　

好吧。我今天晚上打行吗？

　

MARTIN:

Sure.

　

　

当然可以。

　

MOTHER:

OK. Goodbye.

　

　

好的。再见。

　

MARTIN:

Goodbye.

　

　

再见。
