Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 2. First, these greetings（问候语）:

　

Good afternoon.

下午好。

　

I'm happy to meet you.

很高兴见到你。

　

Next, an introduction（介绍）:

　

This is Martin Learner.

这是马丁・露那。

　

These are pronouns（代词）:

　

she / She's an engineer.

她/她是一名工程师。

　

we / We are reporters.

我们/我们是记者。

　

Here is a verb（动词）:

　

meet / met

见面/过去分词

　

These are nouns（名词）:

　

accountant

会计

　

doctor

医生

　

engineer

工程师

　

musician

音乐家

　

reporter

记者

　

Finally, some other phrases from Lesson 2（本课出现的短语）:

　

We've met.

我们见过面。

　

Pardon? (= What did you say?)

对不起？（你说什么？）

　

too / He's a reporter too.

也/他也是一名记者。

　

and / This is Martin and this is Jim.

和/这是马丁，这是吉姆。
