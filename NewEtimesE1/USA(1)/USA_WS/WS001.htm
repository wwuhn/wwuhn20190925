Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 1. First are these greetings（问候语）:

　

Hello.

你好。

　

Good morning.

早上好。

　

How are you?

你身体好吗？

　

Fine thanks.

很好，谢谢。

　

These words are pronouns（代词）:

　

I / I'm Martin Learner.

我/我是马丁・勒纳。

　

you / How are you?

你/你好吗？

　

he / He's a reporter.

他/他是一名记者。

　

These are verbs（动词）:

　

be (is, am, are)

是

　

visit (visiting)

访问

　

meet (meeting)

见面，碰头

　

These are nouns（名词）:

　

airport

飞机场

　

office

办公室

　

reporter

记者

　

Finally, these additional phrases（短语）:

　

Come in.

进来。

　

Please.

请。

　

Thank you.

谢谢。
