Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 3. First, these nouns(名词）:

　

designer

设计者

　

machine operator

机器操作人

　

driver

司机

　

painter

画家

　

grinder

磨工

　

supervisor

监督者，管理者

　

mechanic

机械师

　

welder

焊接工

　

The following nouns are from previous lessons（前课出现名词）:

　

accountant

会计

　

engineer

工程师

　

assistant manager

副部长，经理助理

　

communications director

通信部部长

　

musician

音乐家

　

doctor

医生

　

reporter

记者

　

Next, some words that introduce questions（疑问词）:

　

What / What do you do?

什么/你干什么？

　

How / How are you?

如何/你好吗？

　

These pronouns are used as subjects of sentences（代词）:

　

I / I am an engineer.

我/我是一名工程师。

　

you / You are a grinder.

你/你是一名磨工。

　

he / He is a painter.

他/他是一名画家。

　

she / She is a driver.

她/她是一名司机。

　

it / It is an airport.

它/那是一个机场。
What is that? It is an airport.

　

we / We are welders.

我们/我们都是焊接工。

　

you / You are drivers.

你们/你们是司机。

　

they / They are reporters.

他们/他们是记者。

　

Finally, these questions（疑问句）:

　

What do you do? (= What work do you do? What is your occupation?)

你是是干什么的？（你是干什么工作的？你的职业是什么？）
