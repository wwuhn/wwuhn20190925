Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 27. First, these nouns（名词）:

　

basketball

篮球

symphony

交响曲

　

tennis

网球

　

track

径类运动

　

Here are some verbs used as nouns（名词):

　

jumping

跳跃

　

running

赛跑

　

Next, an adjective（形容词）:

　

popular

大众的 (popular music)

　

Here is a pronoun（代词）:
　 anything 任何
I don't do anything.

　

Finally, some grammar notes（语法说明）:

　

Almost any type of noun can follow the verb "like."

动词like后差不多可加任何名词。

　

I like Jimmy Connors.

我喜欢吉米.康尼。

　

I like tennis.

我喜欢打网球。

　

I like music.

我喜欢听音乐。

　

I like running.

我喜欢跑步。

　

I like Chicago.

我喜欢芝加哥。
