Hi. This is Ray McDonald and Nancy Beardsley with the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 4. First the greeting I just used（问候语）:

　

Hi. (informal, with friends)

嗨。（非正式的，与朋友打招呼）

　

Next some nouns you might use when you talk about work, occupation, or profession（一些与职业有关的名词）:

　

teacher

教师

　

nothing (= no thing)

无（=没有东西）

EXAMPLE: What do you do?  Nothing.

你干什么？没干什么。
　

EXAMPLE: What do you want?  Nothing.

你想要什么？不想要什么。

　

Finally, this phrase（短语）:

　

Please write it for me.

请把它写给我。
