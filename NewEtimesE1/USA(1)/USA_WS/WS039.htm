Hi. This is Nancy Beardsley and Ray McDonald. These are the words and phrases from the English USA worksheet for Lesson 39. First, these nouns:

　

cap

帽子

college

大学

　

football

足球、橄榄球

leg

腿

　

soccer

英式足球

　

Next some adjectives:

　

blonde masculine / blonde feminine

金发男子/金发女子

　

curly

卷曲的

curly hair

　

long

长的

long hair

　

straight

直的

straight hair

　

Finally, these words [sentences] that describe appearance:

描述样貌的词语（句子）：

　

What does he look like?

他长得什么样子？

　

He looks like his brother.

他长得象他的兄弟。

　

He is tall.

他长得高。

　

He has brown hair.

他长着棕色头发。

　

Some are handsome and some are not. / Some are handsome and some are not handsome.

有些人长得英俊而有些人长得并不英俊。

　

Some men are tall and some men are not. / Some men are tall and some men are not tall.

有些人长得高而有些人长得不高。
