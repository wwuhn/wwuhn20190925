Finally, some grammar notes about using the words "may" and "can."

　

The question words "may" and "can" are similar in use, but for many English speakers, they carry different meanings.

"May" asks someone for permission.

疑问词may和can在使用上相似，但两者还是表达不同的意思。

may表示询问以得到许可。

　

May I come in?

我能进来吗？

　

May I go to the party?

我能去那个晚会吗？

　

May he stay all night?

他能呆整晚吗？

　

　

　

"Can" asks whether someone is able.

can表示询问别人的能力。

　

Can you come tomorrow?

你明天能来吗？

　

Can he finish this evening?

他今晚能完成吗？

　

Can you work on Monday?

你星期一能工作吗？
